A Complete Timeline of Emily Ratajkowskis Dating History

Since propelling to the spotlight in 2013, Emily Ratajkowski has had an extensive and near-continuous star-studded love life—particularly following her split from husband Sebastian Bear-McClard.

Over the last decade, the model and My Body author has openly entertained casual and longterm romances with men from all walks of Hollywood. (And, having come out as bisexual in October 2022, she recently shared that she would “love” to soon date a woman.) Meanwhile, her September 2022 divorce from Bear-McClard marked a whole new dating era for the model—one of brief romantic escapades with an array of stars, including actor Brad Pitt, comedians Pete Davidson and Eric André, and most recently, singer Harry Styles.

Ahead, track every person EmRata has dated so far since 2013.

Harry Styles (March 2023)

The two friends went viral online after they were captured on video making out near a parked car in Tokyo, where Ratajkowski had joined Styles on a leg of his Love On Tour show.

Ratajkowski seemingly alluded to the steamy moment with Styles the following month, telling the Los Angeles Times that there are “a million insane, inaccurate things” that have been said about her relationships, but sometimes, “things”—like her kiss with the singer—“just happen.”

“She is having fun and enjoys the attention from guys,” an insider told People following her spotting with Styles in March. Meanwhile, another insider reported to Us Weekly, “Harry has always been very attracted to Emily,” adding that he was “thrilled” about “hooking up” with Ratajkowski.

Eric André (January–February 2023)

Gotham//Getty Images

The model enjoyed a very public monthlong romance with comedian Eric André. The two were first spotted out in Manhattan around New Year’s Eve—and by January, they were captured by Page Six kissing on the beach while on a steamy vacation in the Cayman Islands.

Following a sweet date to a Knicks game on Valentine’s Day, the model and comedian went Instagram official in the most dramatic way possible—with a photo shared to both André's grid and Story of the two posing completely naked. Just several days later, however, Ratajkowski seemingly confirmed her split from André when she shared a video on TikTok referencing a failed situationship.

Jack Greer (December 2022)

Amid ongoing rumors of her split from Davidson, Ratajkowski was spotted out with a new suitor: visual artist Jack Greer. Per photos obtained by People, she and Greer were photographed packing on the PDA in New York City on December 21. The casual outing was Ratajkowski’s first and only captured appearance with the artist before she began seeing comedian André several weeks later.

Pete Davidson (November–December 2022)

Getty Images

Ratajkowski’s short-lived romance with the comedian was not necessarily unexpected (see here for more on the phenomenon that is Davidson's star-studded dating history)—but certainly entertaining for fans. The two were first spotted out holding hands in Brooklyn in November, and over the course of the month, seen on various other dates around New York, including at Davidson’s Brooklyn apartment and at an NBA game at Madison Square Garden.

“Pete makes Emily laugh and he loves how intelligent she is,” an insider told Us Weekly at the time. “Emily thinks Pete is a lot of fun to hang out with,” another source told E! News, who added that the model finds Davidson “charming” and shares “flirtatious chemistry” with him.

BACKGRID

Despite the short-lived fling, the model was charmed by the comedian long before the two got together. One year prior to their romantic stint, Ratajkowski openly praised him during a Late Night appearance, telling host Seth Meyers that she understood Davidson’s appeal. “He seems super charming,” she said candidly at the time. “He’s vulnerable. He’s lovely. His fingernail polish is awesome. He looks good.”

By December 2022, Page Six confirmed that the pair’s romance had fizzled out. (Ratajkowski was already turning heads with her PDA-filled outing with Greer a week prior.) “Their fling has moved into the friend zone,” the insider said, adding that the couple’s new platonic dynamic was “fine with both of them.”

Brad Pitt (September–October 2022)

Soon following her divorce from Bear-McClard, the model was linked to none other than Brad Pitt. At the time, an insider told Us Weekly that the two had been hanging out “as friends”—and while the relationship was never explicitly confirmed as romantic or official, another People source reported that the two spent “a lot of time together.” According to Us, the model and the actor stopped seeing one another in October.

In the interim before seeing Davidson, the model opened up about her newly single lifestyle in Harper’s Bazaar’s November 2022 issue. She said, “I feel all the emotions. I feel anger, sadness. I feel excitement. I feel joy. I feel levity. Every day is different. The only good thing I know is that I’m feeling all those things, which is nice because it makes me believe that I’ll be okay.”

Sebastian Bear-McClard (February 2018–July 2022)

Emma McIntyre /VF20//Getty Images

Less than one month after her split from previous longtime boyfriend Jeff Magid, Ratajkowski went on to swiftly marry film producer Sebastian Bear-McClard, with whom she would stay for the next four and a half years (and welcome a son, Sylvester Apollo Bear).

It remains unconfirmed how exactly Ratajkowski and Bear-McClard first met, but an Us Weekly insider reported in March 2018 that the two shared mutual friends and bonded for two years before embarking on their romance.

MEGA//Getty Images

After just nine days of dating, the model and producer exchanged vows on February 23 in a surprise intimate civil ceremony in New York City. Two months later, on The Tonight Show, Ratajkowski revealed to host Jimmy Fallon that the proposal casually took place at a New York City tavern, and that saying yes to Bear-McClard had been an on-the-spot decision. “I thought about it for about 30 seconds,” she said in April 2018. “It was a city hall courthouse wedding. I guess I didn’t know what I always wanted to do, which is maybe why it was so untraditional.”

In July 2022, Ratajkowski’s romance with Bear-McClard came to an end following rumors of his infidelity. Their divorce was finalized in September 2022.

Jeff Magid (December 2014–January 2018)

Michael Kovac//Getty Images

The model shared one of her first long-term relationships with music producer Jeff Magid, whom she was first linked to in December 2014. Throughout their three-year romance, they were reportedly “inseparable”—living together in downtown L.A. and making various joint appearances, including at Paris Fashion Week and Coachella in 2017.

Ratajkowski and Magid eventually called it quits in the beginning of 2018, reportedly due to career incompatibilities in the wake of his busy traveling schedule and her increasing fame. “Their business success[es] did not help them spend much time together,” a source told the New York Daily News.

Andrew Dryden (circa 2013–February 2014)

Noam Galai//Getty Images

In her first relationship since entering the spotlight, the then-22-year-old model was seen dating Andrew Dryden—cofounder of Los Angeles menswear brand Departamento—whom she presumably met through shared fashion ties.

While not much was reported about their romance, the two were spotted out on multiple occasions throughout their roughly yearlong relationship, namely at fashion shows and after-parties. Their split was confirmed by Ratajkowski on the night of Super Bowl XLVIII, when she reportedly announced at multiple parties that she was single.
