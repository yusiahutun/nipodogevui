Sock it to ‘em: fun and funky socks for spring 2024 – stripes, checks, waves and even leopard-print designs, from Maison Kitsuné, Raey, Thom Browne and Paul Smith

In the realm of fashion, socks have evolved from mere necessities to stylish accessories, capable of elevating any outfit. As we step into the vibrant season of spring in 2024, let’s explore four funky and cool sock designs that are sure to turn heads and add a touch of personality to your wardrobe.

From the playful stripes of Maison Kitsuné to the timeless leopard spots of Raey, the preppy elegance of Thom Browne to the vibrant colours of Paul Smith, these socks are not just for keeping your feet warm, but for making a statement. Get ready to embrace the joy of self-expression through the art of sockology!

‘Bigger than fashion’? Why Taylor Swift will never sign with a luxury brand

1. Maison Kitsuné

French-Japanese lifestyle brand Maison Kitsuné is behind these striped cotton socks in cobalt blue and black. The upper has an elasticated comfort band and a subtle fox patch print.

2. Raey

These timeless leopard-spot socks are from Raey, Matches’ in-house label. We’ll be styling them with everything from casual trainers to chunky winter boots.

4 of this month’s hottest fashion picks – from Cardi B to Frank Gehry

3. Thom Browne

We’re big fans of these preppy mid-calf socks by American designer Thom Browne. Made from wool jacquard, the pair features a stylish tartan pattern and the brand’s signature striped tag, and are priced at a cool US$180.

4. Paul Smith

Paul Smith’s multicoloured socks are made in Italy using a soft cotton blend. They’re detailed with a torn stripe motif and contrasting panels on the ankles, heels and toes.
