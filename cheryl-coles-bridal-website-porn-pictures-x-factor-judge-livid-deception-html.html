Cheryl Cole's fury as her pictures are used for mail order bride website and fake porn shots are auctioned online

A Russian website has used X Factor judge Cheryl Cole to promote their mail order bride business.

Pictures of the Girls Aloud star have been posted on website RussianBrides.org, in which the 27-year-old is apparently one of the many women seeking a husband.

One picture shows the millionaire singer in a wholesome white cardigan over the title of the website, while in another, a headshot of her is shown next to the advice 'assess precisely what it is you are looking for in your Russian bride-to-be'.

Seeking: The Girls Aloud star is apparently one of the women seeking a husband

Advice: Mrs Cole is pictured in a section offering advice to would-be husbands

Despite the fact it seems unlikely that - even after her recent divorce from Ashley Cole -  one of the world's most beautiful and famous women would need to use the internet to find a husband, it comes just a couple of months after it emerged that pictures of Girls Aloud were illicitly used to promote a Russian escort agency.

The Babylon Moscow Escort Agency claimed that Sarah Harding, Cheryl Cole, Nicola Roberts, Nadine Coyle and Kimberley Walsh were among its 'friendly and sophisticated girls waiting for your call'.

However, while the band reportedly saw the funny side, this time the joke has worn thin for Cheryl.

Stunning: Cheryl Cole at the Brits on Tuesday night. A friend said she was absolutely furious as she was extremely careful about the products she endorses

A friend told the Daily Star she would be 'livid', adding: 'She is one of the world's most beautiful women and it's not surprising people want to use her image to attract customers.

'However she is extremely careful about any products she endorses - and this Russian bride agency is most certainly not one of them.

'It is clearly a deception and the plug needs to be pulled on it.'

Porn: Fake photos of a naked Cheryl had been on sale on eBay

Image: The X Factor judge's head was pasted on pornographic pictures and offered for sale

Apology: The website has apologised and removed the fake photos from sale

In another abuse of the X Factor judge's image, doctored photographs of her in X-rated poses have been posted for sale on an auction website.

The snaps which show her naked and wearing skimpy lingerie were produced by scanning her head on to pornographic pictures. The have subsequently been removed by eBay, which has apologised, according to the newspaper.

The boss of Russian Brides, Irina Crebneva said Cheryl's photographs had appeared 'by mistake' and she had not heard of the singer.
