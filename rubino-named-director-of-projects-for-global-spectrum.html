Rubino Named Director Of Projects For Global Spectrum

(CelebrityAccess MediaWire) —

Global Spectrum named Daniel Rubino director of projects. He will consult all elements of corporate projects and support pre-opening activities for Global Spectrum-managed facilities.

"Daniel Rubino brings a diverse background of knowledge from 13 years in the architectural industry and the last eight years in the facility management field," said Global Spectrum Chief Operating Officer John Page. "Our buildings throughout North America and our consulting projects worldwide will rely heavily on his expertise and passion for the industry to continue our rapid growth into the future."

Rubino joins Global Spectrum after serving eight years with Centerplate, which provides catering, concessions, management, and merchandise for facilities. Prior to working in the public assembly facility management industry, Rubino worked in the architectural field for Odell Associates, Inc. from 1985-1997. –Bob Grossweiner and Jane Cohen
