Kendall Jenner embraces ladylike dressing for Easter

Kendall Jenner’s trademark minimalism just got a hint of coquette. Right in time for spring, the supermodel is embracing the girly, romantic aesthetic, signs of which include bows, puffed or frilly sleeves, pastel pinks, ballet flats, vintage-inspired corsets, florals — the list goes on.

Jenner debuted what we’d call an elevated coquette look on Easter Sunday, a celebratory day she spent with family. The piece is a silk off-white maxi dress with a V neck and a pretty, flowy skirt. At the shoulders, puffy short sleeves are embellished with black velvet bows with long ribbons. Meanwhile, subtle seams create a triangle bra detail and separate the skirt from the bodice.

The beautiful dress hails from Rodarte’s spring/summer 2024 collection. Styled by Dani Michelle, Jenner stayed true to her chic style and paired the dress only with pointy black heels, black oval sunglasses, gold hoop earrings, and a gold ring. She wore her dark hair straight down, and had on rosy makeup and a matte brown lip.

The dress – which is available for pre-order now – also comes without sleeves in a yellow hue, while the label also has plenty of beautiful white dresses, and we have rounded up some similar styles below too.

“Dress of my dreams,” Jenner captioned an Instagram post featuring photos of herself in the ensemble. In one, she sits on her white couch; in others, she walks along a path lined with trees.

“My beautiful Princess in her secret garden,” her mother, Kris Jenner commented on the post, later noting on her own Instagram Story that she was the photographer behind the candid shoot. “She’s so beautiful inside and out,” she added on her Story, speaking of her supermodel daughter.

Jenner hosted a lavish luncheon for the whole family this Easter, and gifted her kids and grandkids cosy bunny pyjamas to match hers.
