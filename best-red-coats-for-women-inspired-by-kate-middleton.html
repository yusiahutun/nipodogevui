7 Kate Middleton-worthy red coats to shop this season

There’s no doubt Princess Kate loves a statement coat. From longline pieces in bright red and orange shades to scarlet tweed blazers and bold puffer jackets, she's championed them all.  

On Wednesday, Kate stepped out in Windsor wearing a red tailored Alexander McQueen coat as she joined her husband Prince William at the St David's Day parade. The Princess has several coats in scarlet shades by the designer brand, and has also been pictured in a longline red coat by Catherine Walker.

Princess Kate wears Alexander McQueen in Windsor

Not averse to a high street buy, back in September the mum-of-three was pictured in Wales wearing LK Bennett’s Spencer coat in red. The beautiful wool-blend jacket featured a tailored silhouette and gold hardware - and it's still available to shop.
