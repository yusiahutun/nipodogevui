Meaning of Type Of Way (official Remix) by Meek Mill

The song "Type Of Way (official Remix)" by Meek Mill featuring Jeezy and Rich Homie Quan delves into various themes that contribute to the overall message of the song. This article will dissect the standout lyrics and explore the unexpected and imaginative themes that they help develop.The overall theme of the song revolves around the emotions and reactions people experience when they see others achieving success or living a lavish lifestyle. It explores the feelings of envy, resentment, and even admiration that can arise when one person feels that another has surpassed them in terms of financial success or social status.One standout lyric that contributes to this theme is Jeezy's line, "They like, 'Jizzle, why they hating? They feel some type of way.'" This lyric showcases how people's negative reactions to someone's success often arise from their own feelings of inadequacy or jealousy. It highlights the idea that haters are simply projecting their own insecurities onto others, rather than celebrating their achievements.Another important lyric that develops the theme is Rich Homie Quan's line, "Mr. CEO is what my title say." This line not only emphasizes Quan's own success but also hints at the power dynamics that can come with achieving a high social status. It suggests that people might feel a certain type of way because they aspire to acquire the same level of authority and influence that comes with being a CEO.Furthermore, Meek Mill's verse introduces an unexpected twist to the theme. He raps, "Okay, I popped a nigga chick, and he feel some type of way. Started talking dirty about me, popped up dead right away." Here, Meek Mill explores the darker side of envy and jealousy. He illustrates how some individuals may resort to violence or threats in order to alleviate their own feelings of inferiority. This juxtaposes the extravagant lifestyle depicted in other parts of the song with the harsh reality of violence and retribution.One more interesting lyric that contributes to the overarching theme is Rich Homie Quan's line, "That custom Breitling make you feel some type of way." This line speaks to the idea that material possessions can both incite envy and create a sense of superiority. It suggests that people may feel a certain type of way when they observe others flaunting their expensive watches or designer brands, showcasing the power of material wealth as a status symbol.Overall, the lyrics in "Type Of Way (official Remix)" delve into the complex emotions and reactions that arise when people perceive others to be more successful or influential. The song explores themes of envy, admiration, power dynamics, violence, and materialism. Through unexpected and imaginative metaphors and storylines, the song highlights the intricacies and contradictions involved in navigating the social hierarchies of success.
