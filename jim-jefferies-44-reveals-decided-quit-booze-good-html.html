Jim Jefferies, 44, reveals why he decided to quit booze for good

He's the popular Australian comic who garnered somewhat of a 'bad boy' reputation thanks to his stand-up shows over the years.

But on Sunday, Jim Jefferies admitted he's given his lifestyle a complete overhaul now he's getting older - including giving up booze forever and enjoying a vegan menu thanks to his stunning wife Tasie Lawrence, 30.

Speaking to The Daily Telegraph, the 44-year-old admitted he 'wishes he'd taken better care' of his body in his twenties, but is now doing what he can to be healthy.

Changes: On Sunday, Jim Jefferies, 44, admitted he's changed his ways now he's getting older - including giving up booze and enjoying a 'vegan' lifestyle thanks to his stunning wife Tasie Lawrence. Both pictured

'I don’t drink any longer,' the Los Angeles based star said. 'I actually had the early onset of psoriatic arthritis – I was waking up and my hands were clawed. Then I gave up the booze. It’s not fixed, but it’s 90 per cent better after I did that.'

Jim sweetly added that he thinks he's going to live longer thanks to his wife Tasie, adding: 'I will say this – since marrying my wife I think I’ll live another 10 years because she’s vegan and I eat whatever food she cooks for herself.'

The comic said that while he is changing some parts of his lifestyle, he hasn't done anything too strenuous - and often finds it hard to get out of bed to exercise.

While he won't be bashing out hundreds of sit-ups every day, he's trying to move more because 'if you don’t move, you won’t move.'

And while he eats well these days, there's one food he can't give up - even if it's not particularly healthy.

'I’ll be happy to take a few years off my life if I keep getting to eat pasta,' he added.
