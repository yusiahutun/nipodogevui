Lori Goldstein

Lori Goldstein is a well-known fashion stylist, famous for creating LOGO by Lori Goldstein for a broadcasting TV network called QVC. Goldstein is also an editor as well as an author, known for writing ” Lori Goldstein: Style Is Instinct”. 

Moreover, Goldstein is involved in various advertising campaigns and frequently collaborates with photographers such as Steven Meisel and Annie Leibovitz. Goldstein is also recognized for being the first agency-signed stylist with Art+Commerce.

Who is Lori Goldstein? Her Age and Early Life

The American fashion stylist, Lori Goldstein was born on August 1, 1956, in Columbus, Ohio, USA. As of 2023, she turned 67 years old and her birth sign is Leo. Lori is the daughter of Howie Goldstein and Marcia Goldstein. She grew up with her three siblings; Marla, Vicki, and Eric.

As for her family background, she was born to an Italian-Irish family and mostly spent her childhood days in the New Jersey Shore. For her education and academic achievements, she completed her bachelor’s degree at a recognized university majoring in journalism.

The multi-talented fashion stylist, Lori was so passionate about fashion design from an early age. Her interest in the design field led her to work at Fred Segal, a clothing and accessories retail store in Los Angeles, the United States. While she was accompanying Fred on a buying trip to New York, she decided to move to New York to start her fashion career.
