Top 12 funniest Mexican comedians you should watch in 2024

It is always worthwhile to listen to the funniest Mexican comedians doing stand-up comedy. They use mockery, satire, great storylines, steamy scenes, tense social backdrops, and a ton of heart to make you laugh. The comedians also use humour to address some of the most critical issues in society, including drug-related violence, economic inequality, and political corruption. But regardless of the massive number of Mexican comedians, there is always that one comedian that will make you laugh and make your day.

However, things have changed, and Mexican comedians have invaded live shows, including specials, reality TV, podcasts, scripted series, stage plays, and cameos in theatrical films. But who are the funniest Mexican comedians you should watch in 2024?

You can go to almost any streaming platform and search for Mexican comedians like Sofia Niño de Rivera, Alex Fernández, and Ricardo O’Farrill and listen to them. They can use any topic, including politics, religion, corruption, insecurity, and se*x, and turn it into a burst of humour without offending anybody. However, here are 12 more Mexican comedians worth listening to in 2024.

1. Al Madrigal

Al Madrigal is one of the best Mexican comedians on Comedy Central. He was born Alessandro Liborio Madrigal on 4 July 1971 (age 53 as of 2024) in San Francisco, United States.

Madrigal is the founder of the All Things Comedy podcast network. He rose to fame on The Daily Show with Jon Stewart as a correspondent for five seasons and had a special on Comedy Central titled Why Is The Rabbit Crying? His comedy is story-based and centres around his personal life and family.

2. Anjelah Johnson

Anjelah Johnson was born Anjelah Nicole Johnson-Reyes on 14 May 1982 (42 as of 2024) in San Jose, California, United States. She is an actress, comedian, and former NFL cheerleader well known for being a featured performer on the sketch comedy show MADtv.

In 2009, she had a one-house Comedy Central special titled Anjelah Johnson: That's How We Do It. Her impressions and characters characterise her comedy style, and she often incorporates her personal experiences and cultural background into her comedy routines.

3. Arturo Castro

Arturo Castro is one of the most famous Latino comedians. Although not Mexican, he is Guatemalan and best known for portraying Jaimé Castro on the Comedy Central series Broad City. Arturo was born in Guatemala on 28 November 1985 (age 39 as of 2024). Castro's comedy style focuses more on millennial and Latinx themes. Also, he explores issues related to identity and culture in his work.

4. Dustin Ybarra

Dustin Ybarra is one of the best Mexican stand-up comedians. He is also an actor born on 30 October 1989 (age 35 as of 2024) in San Antonio, Texas, United States. Ybarra is best known for his role as Tyler Medina in the comedy series Kevin (Probably) Saves the World. Like most Mexican comedians, Ybarra performs a variety of comedy styles, ranging from observational humour to character-based comedy.

5. Erik Rivera

Erik Rivera is one of the best and fastest-growing Mexican comedians to listen to in 2024. He debuted in 2015 with a one-hour Comedy Special titled I’m No Expert.

In 2017, he portrayed Rafael in Superior Donuts, where he talked about the current state of immigration in the United States. Erik Rivera also does stand-up comedy. His style of comedy focuses on hilarious truths of parenting and married life but also explores other societal issues.

6. Felipe Esparza

Felipe Esparza is a stand-up comedian and actor born on 11 June 1976 (age 48 as of 2024). He started stand-up comedy in 1994 and won the Last Comic Standing in 2010. Also, Felipe Esparza has hosted a weekly podcast called What's Up Fool? since 2014.

Felipe Esparza is known for his raw, real-life comedy that audiences can relate to regardless of race. He often draws on his experiences as a Mexican-American growing up in poverty, touching on topics such as immigration, family life, and his struggles with addiction. His humour is self-deprecating, and he can find humour in the darkest situations.

7. Franco Escamilla

Franco Escamilla was born Franco Javier López Escamilla on 29 April 1981 (age 43 as of 2024) in Cuautla, Morelos, Mexico. He is one of the best Mexican comedians of 2024, and he primarily performs in the Mexican Republic.

Franco has four specials on Netflix: Comedians of the World, For the Anecdote, Welcome to the World, and Eavesdropping. As a stand-up comedian, Franco is known for his dark and biting sense of humour and has a genuine and relatable style that makes it feel like you are talking to a friend.

8. Fran Hevia

Fran Hevia was born Francisco Agustín Rosales Hevia on 11 August 1986 (age 38 as of 2024) in the State of Mexico. Hevia is based in Mexico and has participated in many Mexican programmes, such as Comedy Central Presenta and Comedy Central Stand Up. Also, he appeared in six seasons of La Culpa es de Cortés on Comedy Central for all of Latin America. In 2016, his comedy special Se me hació fácil became the first Spanish-speaking stand-up comedy album, reaching the gold record distinction.

9. Gabriel Iglesias

Gabriel Iglesias is a big Mexican comedian known worldwide. He was born Gabriel Jesus Iglesias in San Diego, California, United States, on 15 July 1976 (age 48 as of 2024). Iglesias has done many stand-up specials for Netflix and Comedy Central.

His most notable one is the I'm Not Fat… I'm Fluffy and Hot & Fluffy comedy special. Gabriel Iglesias' style of comedy is a mixture of storytelling with characters and sound effects that brings his personal experiences to life.

10. Melissa Villaseñor

Melissa Villaseñor is one of the most multitalented female Mexican comedians to watch in 2024. She was born Melissa Anne Villaseñor on 9 October 1987 (age 36) in Whittier, California, United States.

Melissa is well known for being a semi-finalist in America’s Got Talent (2011). But she has also appeared on the NBC sketch comedy series Saturday Night Live. While her comedy style includes impressions of famous people, her performances are also noted for their upbeat and cheery persona.

11. Sofia Niño de Rivera

Sofia Niño de Rivera Mesta was born on 23 November 1981 (age 43 as of 2024) in Mexico City, Mexico. She is arguably the best female Mexican comedian of all time.

Sofia is often credited as the leading exponent of stand-up comedy in Mexico and Latin America and was the first woman to get a Spanish-language Netflix comedy special. Her comedy revolves around telling personal stories and jokes about various topics, including her family, relationships, and modern life.

12. Tom Segura

Tom Segura is one of the best male Hispanic comedians to watch in 2024. He was born Thomas Weston Segura on 16 April 1979 (age 45 as of 2024) in Cincinnati, Ohio, United States. Segura is the co-host of the Your Mom's House and the Two Bears One Cave podcasts. Tom Segura does observational comedy, black comedy, sarcasm, insult comedy, and deadpan humour.
