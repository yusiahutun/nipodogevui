Joy Corrigan showcases her figure in a TINY bra top and matching thong as she fills up her car

She never shies away from flaunting her jaw-dropping figure.  

And Joy Corrigan made no exception as she filled up her car at a gas station on Wednesday wearing a tiny bra top and matching thong. 

The Sports Illustrated swimsuit model, 28, flashed her taut abs in the bright pink two-piece as she seductively leaned against her SUV. 

She teamed the swimwear with a cropped jacket, that featured sporty side stripes, and a pair of matching joggers. 

Joy boosted her height with some glitzy, silver heeled boots and pulled down her bottoms to make sure her midriff was on full display.

Sensational: The Sports Illustrated swimsuit model flashed her taut abs in the bright pink two-piece as she seductively leaned against her SUV

The model accessorised with a pink pink handbag and shielded her eyes with some sunglasses. 

She wore her blonde hair down and styled the front pieces into two neat plaits as she continued to channel her festival look from Coachella. 

Joy's outing comes after she turned heads once again as she strolled through the desert in a very daring ensemble while attending the festival in Indio, California, over the weekend.

For the event, she wore a semi-sheer lace bra top with a matching skirt and wide brimmed hat. 

The model was among the roughly 250,000 attendees who flocked to the highly-popular festival this year to watch Bad Bunny, Blackpink and Frank Ocean perform.

Other artists who performed at the festival were Calvin Harris, Icelandic star Bjork, Spain's Rosalia, Nigeria's Burna Boy and British-Indian singer Jai Paul.  

Kaytranada, Porter Robinson, Yaeji and vets Chemical Brothers, Underworld and Sasha & John Digweed were also there. 

Alt acts were Gorillaz, Wet Leg, Boygenius (featuring Phoebe Bridgers, Lucy Dacus and Julien Baker), Charli XCX, Remi Wolf, Ethel Cain, Christine and the Queens, Weyes Blood, Muna, Snail Mail, Sudan Archives and veterans Blondie and the Breeders.

Bad Bunny is one of the world's most popular artists and in 2022, he earned as much as $435.38 million from his 'World's Hottest Tour'.

The rap star, 29, actually surpassed the previous record, which was set by Ed Sheeran for his Divide tour in 2018, when he earned $432.3 million.

Coachella takes place at the Empire Polo Grounds in Indio, California on the weekends of April 14-16 and April 21-23.

Last year's headliners were Harry Styles, Billie Eilish, and the Weeknd as well as Swedish House Mafia. Doja Cat, Lil Baby, Phoebe Bridgers, Megan Thee Stallion, 21 Savage were there too.
