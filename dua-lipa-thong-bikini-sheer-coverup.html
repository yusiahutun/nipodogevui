Dua Lipa Paired a Thong Bikini with a Sheer Beach Coverup

Dua Lipa touched down in Miami just a day ago, and of course her wardrobe is already pure chaos. On Saturday, the pop star stepped out in two eclectic looks — first at the beach, and then for a late-night dinner.

She started off her day at the beach, and wore a black thong bikini beneath a sheer, green space-dye coverup (though, it didn't cover much) that matched her emerald manicure and pedicure. The knit dress featured a massive cutout at the midriff and tied around her neck. Dua added '90s throwback platform sandals and blingy sunglasses to her look. Making the case for jewelry at the beach, she accessorized with silver hoops, a Cartier watch, and colorful rings on both hands.

Hours later, Dua changed up her look for dinner, and the look was arguably more chaotic than the previous one. Wearing multiple prints in a single outfit, she combined a multi-colored bandana top with a checkered top and striped bottom with a pair of ombre jeans emblazoned with hand-painted birds. A bright blue leather trench coat slung off her shoulders, while a chunky, statement butterfly necklace, red purse, and black pointed-toe heels provided the final touches.

Dua's upcoming Future Nostalgia tour kicks off in Miami next week, and days ago, Dua stopped by The Late Show to discuss how the delay of the pandemic gave her time to refine her performance plans. "It's massively different," the singer-songwriter revealed about how ideas for the tour evolved. "This time I've done a lot of audience research and I feel like I know what songs people like…it's the very best of."
