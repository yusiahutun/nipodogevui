Short Bio of Carolyn Manno: Wiki

Carolyn Manno is born on16th July 1984 (32 years of age) in Florida, USA. She graduated from the University of Florida by measuring in Telecommunications.

After her graduation, she focused her career in reporting various sports events especially football games. She got a break in her reporting career when she got an opportunity to cover the news for Notre Damn Fighting Irish, Clubs and White Sox, Chicago Bears, Indy 500 and Indianapolis Colts in the South Bend.

After working for South Bend for around three years, the lady resigned from the South Bend and joined the Comcast Sports Net, New England where she covered the news for The Super Bowl, Stanley Cup, and The NBA Finals.

In the year 2009, she left the sports channel and joined NBC Sports channel. Her talent and smartness helped the lady to achieve more in her career. After joining NBC sports, she got an opportunity to host Olympic Games in the year 2012 and later covered important sports events for NBL, NFL, NHL.

The lady due to her extreme talent has received awards and honor including Edward R. Murrow Award and the Indian Spectrum Award. Currently, she is working for NBC Sports Network, and hosts show Football Night in America.
