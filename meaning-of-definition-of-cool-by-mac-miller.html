Meaning of Definition Of Cool by Mac Miller

The Mac Miller song titled 'Definition Of Cool' is a catchy and energetic track that boasts a strong sense of self-confidence and swagger. The overall theme of the song revolves around Mac Miller's personal definition of cool and his belief in his own greatness. He exudes a confident and almost cocky attitude throughout the song, proclaiming himself to be the epitome of cool.One standout lyric in the song is "I'm the future, you a fake, Miss Cleo." This lyric conveys Mac Miller's conviction that he is ahead of his time and destined for greatness. He sees himself as a visionary who can see through the facade of others who claim to be cool but lack authenticity. This develops the theme of personal authenticity and the importance of staying true to oneself.Another notable lyric is "All my bitches bad like Shaq shootin' free throws." This line may initially seem superficial, but it actually touches on the theme of confidence and attractiveness. Mac Miller compares his female companions to Shaq, one of the greatest basketball players of all time, effortlessly sinking free throws. This suggests that Mac Miller surrounds himself with confident and successful individuals, further solidifying his own coolness.Mac Miller also mentions his association with J Dilla, a renowned producer, by rapping "Get my lyrics from L and my soul from J Dilla." This line not only pays homage to J Dilla's musical influence but also explores the theme of artistic inspiration and the impact it has on one's creativity. It suggests that Mac Miller draws inspiration from other talented artists, fueling his own creative process and contributing to his definition of coolness.Additionally, the lyric "Go and stay in Bel-Air like Uncle Phil do, Get rich, go and buy me a mansion" alludes to the popular television show "The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air" and its iconic character, Uncle Phil. This lyric touches on the theme of success and material wealth, highlighting Mac Miller's aspirations for luxury and financial success as symbols of coolness. It also emphasizes the idea of creating a comfortable and lavish lifestyle for oneself and one's loved ones.Overall, 'Definition Of Cool' delves into various themes such as authenticity, confidence, artistic inspiration, success, and material wealth, all through the lens of Mac Miller's personal definition of cool. The song is an ode to self-confidence and the belief in one's own greatness, encouraging listeners to embrace their individuality and strive for their own definition of coolness.
