Kim Kardashian's Birthday Party Included a Kylie Jenner Makeover

  • Kim Kardashian West celebrated her birthday this weekend (the reality icon turns 39 on October 21).
  • For the special occasion, makeup mogul Kylie Jenner did her sister's makeup.
  • Kim's party took place in Kris Jenner's La Quinta mansion near Palm Springs.

Kim Kardashian is making the most of having the founder of Kylie Cosmetics in the family.

In order to celebrate her 39th birthday, Kim enlisted the help of an extra special makeup artist to create the perfect party look. Sharing video footage of the makeover on Instagram, Kim revealed that her little sister, Kylie Jenner, was responsible for doing her makeup.

In the sweet video, Kylie can be seen applying blusher to Kim's cheeks, and creating a nude-inspired makeup look. The KKW Beauty founder also jokes about the fact that Kylie is still baking her own makeup, and has powder all over her face, while she works.

Meanwhile, the mansion was decorated for Kim's party. With an array of cute table settings and chic peach balloons, letters in the center of the table spelled out the message, "Happy Kim Day."

Despite the party taking place in Kris Jenner's residence, the momager was out of town, as she attended Jennifer Lawrence's wedding to Cooke Maroney in Rhode Island. Other celebs spotted at the nuptials included Emma Stone, Adele, Sienna Miller, Nicole Richie, and Ashley Olsen. The newlyweds also threw a rehearsal dinner clambake the night before their big day on the secluded Rose Island.
