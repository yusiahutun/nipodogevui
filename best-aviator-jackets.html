1

Best Vintage-Inspired Aviator Jacket

Simon Miller Jetz Faux Shearling Jacket

Inspired by the brand’s best selling Jetz coat, Simon Miller's jacket version has been cropped and fitted for a shrunken feel. An oversized ‘70s inspired collar and vegan suede patchwork detailing give this piece the perfect worn-in look. Everyone will likely assume it's vintage and will definitely be shocked to learn you didn't have to dig through endless vintage sale racks to score it.

The brand's look book has us inspired to try some more funky styling with an acid colored knit dress but you can always go for a more traditional look too with a cable knit sweater and denim flares.

Size Range: XS-XL

Colorways: Choco brown

Materials: Polyester/spandex

4

Best Oversized Aviator Jacket

Acne Studios Leather-Trimmed Shearling Jacket

Acne Studios’ oversized shape has become a brand signature and is always produced each season. Featuring a looser fit and lots of buckle and zipper details, the jacket is an investment worth making. Celebs love this jacket too, and it's been spotted on everyone from Alexa Chung to Kendall Jenner. But be warned: They regularly sell out quickly, so make sure to get your hands on one now while it's still available.

Wear yours with basics, like a knitted turtleneck and jeans, or a flowy floral dress and boots, for endless styling opportunities.

Size Range: 32FR-42FR

Colorways: Black

Materials: 100% lamb shearling body; 100% calf leather trim; 100% cotton lining

Best Boxy Aviator Jacket

Isabel Marant Étoile Leather and Shearling Jacket

5

Best Boxy Aviator Jacket

Isabel Marant Étoile Leather and Shearling Jacket

You can count on Isabel Marant to offer a military-inspired jacket that is at the top of our must-have list year after year. This season’s aviator style is boxy and cropped with an asymmetrical zip front and leather wrapped collar buttons. Essentially it has that distinctively Parisian feel that is synonymous with the brand. When it comes to styling, this jacket will go just as great with a feminine skirt and knitwear as it will with jeans and a T-shirt.

Size Range: 34FR-42FR

Colorways: Camel and ecru

Materials: 100% lamb shearling

Best Sustainable Aviator Jacket

Make the style your own by mixing with your favorite wardrobe staples, like cashmere crewnecks and boyfriend jeans.

Colorways: Black

Materials: 100% polyester self; 100% polyurethane contrast fabric; 100% recycled polyester lining
