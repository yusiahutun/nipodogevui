Professional Career

Robert Desiderio rose to fame in daytime dramas including “Search for Tomorrow” (1979-80), “Ryan’s Hope” (1982), and “One Life to Live” (1982-83). He appeared in the ABC primetime drama series “Heart of the City” from 1986 to 1987.

From 1988 to 1989, he played Ted Melcher in the CBS primetime soap series “Knots Landing.” In addition, he was nominated for Outstanding Villain: Prime Time at the Soap Opera Digest Awards.
