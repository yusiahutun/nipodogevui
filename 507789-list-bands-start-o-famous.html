1. One Republic

One Republic is one of the most renowned American pop-rock bands. The band was formed in Colorado Springs, Colorado, in 2002. The members of the band are Ryan Tedder (lead vocals, piano), Zach Filkins (lead guitar, viola), Drew Brown (rhythm guitar), Brent Kutzle (bass, cello), Eddie Fisher (drums), and Brian Willett (keyboards, violin).

One thing One Republic is that although they are mostly affiliated with pop-rock, they don't limit themselves to the genre. Ryan Tedder even stated that they don't respect genres and can go for any song or a good artist they consider good to work with.

One Republic achieved its first commercial success on Myspace as an unsigned act. In 2007, the band also achieved massive financial success with their debut album, Dreaming Out Loud. One of the songs in the album, Apologize, was remixed by Timbaland and got international attention. The band’s fifth studio album, Human, released in 2021, sold over 16 million records worldwide.

2. Oasis

Oasis is one of the best rock bands that start with 'O'. The English rock band was formed in Manchester in 1991. It was originally known as Rain and initially consisted of Liam Gallagher (lead vocals, tambourine), Paul Arthurs (guitar), Paul McGuigan (bass guitar), and Tony McCarroll (drums). Noel later joined the band, becoming the fifth and final member.

The Swindon Oasis Leisure Centre inspired the band's name in the Southwest of England. Oasis' 1994 album, Definitely Maybe, under the independent record label Creation Records, sold millions of records, becoming the fastest-selling album in Britain at the time.

Oasis’s success is incredible. The band is among the most successful on the UK Singles Chart and Album Chart, with eight UK number-one singles and eight UK number-one albums.

Although the group parted ways in 2009, Oasis remains heavily influential in British music and culture.

O.A.R. (Of A Revolutions) is an American rock band formed in 1996 in Rockville, Maryland. The band’s members are vocalist/guitarist Marc Roberge, drummer Chris Culos, guitarist Richard On, bassist Benj Gershman, saxophonist/guitarist Jerry DePizzo and touring members.

The band gained attention and developed a strong reputation while performing live university campus performances. O.A.R. gained even more recognition with their hit, Love, and Memories, after partnering with Lava Records in 2005. Their newest album is The Arcade, released on July 15, 2022.

4. Of Montreal

Among the band names that start with 'O', Of Montreal is unique because of its genre. The sophisticated American indie pop band was formed by frontperson Kevin Barnes in 1996. The Of Montreal is named after a failed romance between Barnes and a woman of Montreal.

The band’s music at the beginning was influenced mainly by traditional indie culture, hallucinogenic songwriting, vaudeville, and concert hall. However, they have shifted in their recent releases and adopted other music genres like electronica, indie rock, funk, and dub.

5. Onyx

Onyx is a hip-hop group formed in South Jamaica, Queens, New York City, in 1988 by Fredro Starr. The band is renowned for its unique rap music style and 1993 platinum hit single, Slam, which introduced slam dancing into hip-hop.

Current members of the group include Fredro Starr (1988-present) and Sticky Fingaz (1991-present). Big Dis joined the group in 1988 but died in 2003. Sonny Saeza joined in 1988 but left in 2009.

Even though the music group’s prevalence isn’t as it was in the 90s, they are still popular. Onyx released their ninth album, Onyx 4 Life, on April 9, 2021. The album featured various rappers, including Mad Lion, Cappadonna, Panama P.I., Planet Asia, SickFlo, and Snak the Ripper.
