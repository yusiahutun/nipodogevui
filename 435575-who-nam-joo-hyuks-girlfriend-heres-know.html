Who is Nam Joo Hyuk?

Nam Joo Hyuk is a South Korean actor and model born on February 22, 1994, in South Korea. He is 27 years old as of 2021.

The actor attended Gyeongnam Middle School, where he played basketball and dreamed of becoming a professional basketball player. He played the sport for the school team for three years.
