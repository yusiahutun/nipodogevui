Zelda Williams on Coping With Father Robin Williams Death: I Didnt See Daylight for a While

Zelda Williams is getting candid about how she’s coped with her father Robin Williams‘ death — and revealed she took things day-by-day immediately after the star’s tragic 2014 suicide.

“I just kept going, ‘OK, well today I’m going to wake up and love what I do. And then tomorrow I’m going to wake up and be happy and love what I do. And then the next day.’ Because that’s all you can do,” the 27-year-old actress said during a recent appearance on Chelsea Handler‘s new Netflix talk show, Chelsea.

Zelda and Robin in November 2011.

Zelda admitted she felt most comfortable being alone following Robin’s death on Aug. 11, 2014 and thus spent a tremendous amount of time by herself. “But then also you’re like, ‘Is there something wrong with me?'” she joked.

“I didn’t see daylight for awhile. So now I’m doing a lot more of that. I’ve been working really hard and it’s been nice because you get to not sit with yourself for too long,” she added, according to People.
