Low in Calories

One of the key benefits of La Marca Prosecco is its low calorie content. With just 80 calories per 5-ounce serving, it is a guilt-free choice for those watching their calorie intake.

Zero Fat

La Marca Prosecco is a fat-free beverage, making it a great option for those looking to consume fewer fat grams in their diet.

Low in Sugar

This sparkling wine contains only minimal amounts of sugar, with approximately 1.5 grams per serving. It’s a healthier alternative to sugary cocktails and sweetened beverages.

Rich in Antioxidants

La Marca Prosecco is made from Glera grapes, which are known to be rich in antioxidants. Antioxidants help protect the body from harmful free radicals.

La Marca Prosecco is made with carefully selected, all-natural ingredients. It does not contain any artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives.
