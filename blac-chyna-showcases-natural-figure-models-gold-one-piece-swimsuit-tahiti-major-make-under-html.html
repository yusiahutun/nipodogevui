Blac Chyna showcases natural figure as she models gold one-piece swimsuit in Tahiti... after major m

Blac Chyna has made it a point to showcase her natural hourglass figure after recently undergoing a major make-under and she did so again on Friday as she dropped a series of stunning images on her Instagram.

The model, 35, shared two Instagram posts that contained multiple photos of her modeling a golden one-piece swimsuit from Fashion Nova that showed off her incredible natural figure. 

Chyna, whose birth name is Angela Renée White also shared a Instagram video post that gave a behind-the-scenes look from her photoshoot. 

'You create your own opportunities... Hair: @hearts.pure.brand,' Chyna wrote in the caption of her first Instagram post.

The 35-year-old completed her look with a pair of golden strappy high heels and had a fresh manicure done on her fingernails, which were done in nude-colored acrylics.

All three of Chyna's August 4 Instagram posts showed her doing various modeling poses on a wooden patio by a pool full of green lily pods. The photoshoot was done somewhere in Tahiti, as the model tagged the French Polynesian Island as the location in her first Instagram post. 

It turns out that the photoshoot was a paid partnership with the luxury hair brand Hearts Pure as the mother of two was wearing the company's Full Lace Wig Body Wave 250 percent Density Wig as part of her hair. 

In the first two photos from her first Instagram post, a gold colored brief case featuring Hearts Pure's logo in the center of it, was seen directly behind Chyna as she sat slightly up and ran her finger through her hair in the first image and laid down sideways in the second photo. 

The other images from her first Instagram post contained motivational quotes tailored towards her that were placed in front of black backgrounds. One of the quotes read 'You were born to be real, Angel White, not perfect.'

Another quote read, 'I refuse to let anyone steal Angela White's confidence or happiness.'

Meanwhile, the second Instagram photo dump posted by the 35-year-old was seven photos, with most of them showing her doing various model poses in the gold one-pice swimsuit. The golden briefcase from Hearts Pure is visible in the photos.

'Cherish these moment, while it’s still life in you. @fashionnova,' Chyna, wrote in the caption of her second Instagram post.

In addition to the four swimsuit photos, she also included two images of  motivational quotes, including one that read 'You have one life to live. Make sure it's as wonderful and unique as you are.'

Chyna has two children who each have a different father. Her eldest child is son King Cairo, 10, from her relationship with the rapper Tyga (the former couple were together from 2011 to 2014). 

Meanwhile, her youngest child is her daughter Dream, six, from her relationship with ex-fiancé Rob Kardashian (the couple were together January 2016 to February 2017). 

The model's August 3 Instagram posts come a few days after it was confirmed that she would be one of the 14 celebrities competing on the second season of Fox's Special Forces: World's Toughest Test.

Chyna will face-off against the likes of former NFL player Dez Bryant, Vanderpump Rules star Tom Sandoval, and dancer and singer JoJo Siwa as they push themselves to the limit to survive real military training.

The new season of Special Forces will premiere Monday, September 25 on Fox.
