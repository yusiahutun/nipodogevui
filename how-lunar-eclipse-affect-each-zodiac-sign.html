The Lunar Eclipse in Taurus Will Affect Each Zodiac Sign 2023

A personal journey you began in 2021 is about to come full-circle. Pay attention to how October’s full moon lunar eclipse will affect each zodiac sign, because it will bring you to the final conclusion of this story. Themes of commitment, financial security, and self-reliance are likely at the top of your mind, as unexpected breakthroughs are encouraging you to move forward in a positive and self-enriching manner.

How have your commitments and personal values evolved and transformed in the last couple of years? What is no longer fruitful or stable in your life? This month’s full-moon-lunar-eclipse will take place on October 28 at exactly 4:24 p.m. ET, via 5 degrees Taurus. In addition to everything you’ve accomplished and realized, this eclipse is highlighting what lacks a practical, consistent, and grounded foundation in your life. What do you need to strengthen, solidify, and commit to in order for you to live your best life? What are you willing to live without in order to receive something even better in the long run? Do not fear—this eclipse is leading you toward your ultimate destiny.

This is the final eclipse of 2023, bringing the Taurus-Scorpio eclipse series that started way back in 2021 to a close. Where does this astrological axis exist in your birth chart? The astrological houses occupied by these zodiac signs speak to your personal stability and shared resources. Taurus is ruled by Venus—planet of beauty, security, and self-worth. On the other hand, Scorpio is ruled by Pluto and Mars, symbolizing everything from the way you regenerate after trauma to the way you embrace and accept intimacy. After all, the relationship you have with yourself (Taurus) is a prelude to the relationship you have with others (Scorpio).

This full moon is not just like any other full moon, but something much stronger and more life-changing. A lunar eclipse takes place when the Earth casts its shadow on the moon, making it a more intense and transformative experience. This marks an emotional turning point, as it will usher in life-altering changes between now and the next six months. As for the astrology surrounding this eclipse, it is both auspicious and supportive, thanks to the benevolent influence of Jupiter retrograde in Taurus. The Greater Benefic planet is symbolic of expansion, opportunity, and spiritual growth; you could experience these new energies before and/or after it stations direct in December.

Love planet Venus—the celestial ruler of this eclipse—is currently transiting the methodical and practical sign of Virgo. This is highlighting everything from our daily devotions to the way we maximize our routines. Do your day-to-day affairs contribute to your sense of security? Do they make you more self-reliant? Is there something that needs to be tweaked or reconsidered? This is an opportunity to integrate a deeper mindfulness when it comes to our craft, thanks to Venus in Virgo. Venus will eventually meet with innovative Uranus in a productive trine after the eclipse, bringing forth deeper insights and unveiling game-changing solutions for the future.
