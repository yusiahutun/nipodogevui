    How to Choose the Best Drugstore Face Mask?

    Below are the parameters to keep in mind before buying a drugstore face mask—

    a) Ingredients- Organic ingredients like aloe, cucumber, shea butter, argan oil, African clay, and charcoal work excellently well on all skin types. On the other hand, active ingredients like niacinamide, hyaluronic acid, vitamin B3, AHA, BHA, and vitamin E are what you should look for in your face mask to get the desired results.

    b) Mask Type- There are different types of masks available in the market – sheet masks, gel masks, overnight masks, clay masks, etc. Pick yours considering your needs and skin condition. A sheet mask is usually used daily or on alternative days to get a healthy glow, while a gel mask is excellent for infusing hydration. On the other hand, overnight masks are mostly like moisturizers that you have to apply on your face before sleeping. However, a clay mask should be applied once or twice a week at most.

    c) Skin Type- Consider your skin type and concerns before purchasing a face mask. If you have oily skin, pick a detox face mask that will extract impurities from your pores. If you have dry skin, pick a hydrating mask that will infuse moisture into your skin. For irritated skin, pick a hypoallergenic or soothing mask, which will calm down the redness.

    Now here's how you can incorporate the best drugstore face mask in your skincare routine—

    How to Effectively Use a Drugstore Face Mask?

    Below are the steps to follow when using a drugstore face mask—

    Step 1- First thing first, start by removing all your makeup and sunscreen with a cleansing wipe, balm, or micellar water.

    Step 2- Now, take your face wash as part of the second step of your cleansing routine, apply it generously on your face, and massage it for 60 minutes before washing it off.

    Step 3- Now, in this step, you can use a mild exfoliator to ensure a smooth surface.

    Step 4- Now, based on your preference and skin type, take your mask, and apply it over your face in a way that there is no gap!

    Step 5- This step is optional, you can take a toner and apply it all over your face after patting dry. 

    Step 6- You can also apply a couple of drops of a serum that closely addresses your skin ailments.

    Step 7- Now, take your moisturizer and apply it all over your face, and you are done!

    FAQ's

    Why choose a drugstore face mask over high-end alternatives?

    If a drugstore face mask works like high-end ones, why spend extra pennies? There are drugstore face masks that work like magic on your skin without burning a hole in your pockets.

    How often should I use a drugstore face mask for optimal results?

    You should use it once or twice a week to see visible results. If it is a sheet mask, using it daily will give more benefits.

    Are drugstore face masks suitable for all skin types?

    Yes, drugstore face masks suit all skin types! However, you need to check the formulations, and see what works best for you, before investing in one!

    Can drugstore face masks address specific skincare concerns, such as acne or dryness?

    Yes, there are face masks that are ideally formulated to infuse hydration into the skin, and these are excellent for dryness. There are also face masks that detoxify your skin to help address acne problems.

    How do I know which drugstore face mask is right for my skin type?

    First, you have to identify your skin type! Then, look for a hydrating mask, if you have dry skin. Pick a pore-cleansing mask, if you have oily skin. Lastly, look for a hypoallergenic mask, if you have sensitive skin.

    Can I use multiple face masks in my skincare routine?

    Yes, you can use multiple face masks to address different skin concerns. For oily skin, you can pick a detoxifying and pore-cleansing mask. Similarly, dry skin people can choose a hydrating and glow mask.

    Are there specific drugstore face masks for sensitive skin?

    There are numerous drugstore face masks available for sensitive skin, one of them is the Cetaphil Purifying Clay Mask.

    How long should I leave a drugstore face mask on my face?

    It depends on the product! Check the instructions on the packaging before proceeding. To note, a drugstore face mask can ideally be left for 10-15 minutes.
