#969 Cotton Blues. Laurel Mercantile

Since we’re throwing Jennifer and Kyle a supper club baby shower tomorrow night, it dawned on Mallorie and I that we’d need a gift to bring which resulted in a Hattiesburg trip to Target.

We were thinking on what to have for dinner when we remembered that the much talked about Cotton Blues restaurant would be open now, so we gave it a try and absolutely loved it.

It’s sort of like a fancy Ajax with all local ingredients. So delicious, with some quirky twists on classic Southern dishes. You ought to try it. It’s all good.
