Youll Never Look At Meghan Markle The Same After Seeing This Optical Illusion Photo Of Her With B

Can you imagine Meghan Markle with — wait for it — blonde hair?! Since the Duchess of Sussex started dating Prince Harry back in 2016, the world has become obsessed with Meg’s impeccable style, gorgeous features, and stunning brunette tresses. So it’s no surprise that when an optical illusion photo showing Meghan with blonde hair recently went viral, everyone freaked out. 

When the 37-year-old and her hubby, Harry, 33, stepped out to attend the Prince’s childhood friend Charlie Van Straubenzee’s wedding to Daisy Jenks on Aug. 4, Meghan looked perfectly polished in a color-blocked Club Monaco dress and donned a matching Philip Treacy fascinator atop her signature wavy hair. See the optical illusion pic below!

During the special event, nearby papparazzi snapped tons of photos of Meghan but in one particular pic, the Duchess’ face perfectly aligned with a blonde woman’s sunny locks as Meghan walked by, giving us the ultimate optical illusion of what Meg looks like with blonde hair. 

Even though the Suits star’s long brown hair can still be seen on one side of her face, the other side of the pic seemingly shows Meghan sporting a sprig of blonde hair. And though the snapshot gives her a weird, half-and-half dye job, we’re totally loving what Meg looks like with lighter locks! 

In a recent interview with Refinery 29, Meghan’s former hairstylist Lydia F. Sellers interestingly revealed that Meg’s personal hairstyle hasn’t really changed much since she’s become a royal. “Even now, her look has stayed the same. She’ll throw her hair back in a low bun and it actually looks like she’s done it herself, and it’s beautiful and chic because she’s so confident. That’s the great thing about Meghan — she’s so confident with herself and her look, and she sticks with that.” Maybe one day she’ll stray from her signaure look and actually go blonde!
