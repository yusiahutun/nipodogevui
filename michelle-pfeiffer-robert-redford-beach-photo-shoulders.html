Michelle Pfeiffer stuns fans with flirty photo frolicking with shirtless Robert Redford

Michelle Pfeiffer has been happily married for 26 years, but it wasn't her husband, David E. Kelley's shoulders she was sitting on in one of her infamous Instagram posts. 

The Stardust actress, 62, sent fans wild when she posted a throwback photo with a hunky looking Robert Redford - now 84 - who was baring his chest for the camera. 

Michelle was sat on his shoulders and smiling for the shot which was taken in the sea.
