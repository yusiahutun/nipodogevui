Coldplay Break Attendance Record At Mexico City Show

MEXICO CITY, Mexico (CelebrityAccesss) — Coldplay's "A Head Full of Dreams" tour set a new attendance record at Mexico City's sprawling Foro Sol complex.

The band's previous appearances at the venue on April 15th and 16th set attendance records for consecutive nights culminating in Sunday's record breaking show, show producer Live Nation said.

The previous record was set by Pearl ^ Jam Los Fabulosos Cadillacs in November 2015.

The band's Mexico City dates capped off a successful Latin American tour for the band, who sold out 10 stadium dates, performing in front of more than half a million fans.

Next, the band is heading across the pond to kick off the European leg of their tour with a show in Nice, France on April 24th.

Coldplay is touring in support of "A Head Full of Dreams" their seventh studio album. – Staff Writers
