How Kourtney Kardashian Responded to Celeste Barber Mocking Nude Photo

Comedian Celeste Barber brought up Kourtney's "talent" and the Kardashian's response was gold.

If there's one thing to be said about the Kardashians, they've all perfected the clapback.

Earlier this week, Kourtney Kardashian went totally nude on her Instagram page to announce an upcoming project called Poosh. While nobody really knows what the hell Poosh is, Kourtney's first photo promoting it sure made waves, as she posed completely naked in her bathroom.

The photo caught the eye of comedian Celeste Barber, who has made it part of her brand to recreate popular magazine and fashion shoots starring other -- usually female -- celebrities, with her own twist. Her past targets have included Bella Hadid, Beyonce, Justin Bieber, Cardi B, Demi Lovato and Rihanna.

For this one, Barber went topless in her own bathroom, substituting Kourtney's teacup for a bottle of wine. While Kourt's vanity looked picture perfect, Celeste's was certainly much more realistic.

"When you're flat out trying to keep the world interested in your talent," Barber captioned the photo, along with the hashtags #celestechallengeaccepted #celestebarber #funny and #kourtneykardashian. While some of her followers thought she was simply making fun of herself, others saw it as shade toward the reality TV star.

Kardashian, however, seemed to take it in jest, responding with her own witty comeback in Barber's comments. "When you've got so much talent, might as well share it," she wrote, along with a wink emoji.
