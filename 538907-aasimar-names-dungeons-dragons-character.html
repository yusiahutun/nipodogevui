What is the Aasimar naming convention?

Aasimar does not adhere to fixed naming conventions; instead, their names are usually influenced by the customs and traditions of the societies they belong to. Consequently, their names can vary significantly, mirroring the cultures and regions where they were raised rather than adhering strictly to celestial norms.
