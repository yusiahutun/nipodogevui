Bradley Cooper's Daughter Makes Her Adorable Red Carpet Debut

Bradley Cooper and Irina Shayk’s six-year-old daughter is officially red carpet ready!

Lea De Seine joined her dad on the red carpet Tuesday night for a special Los Angeles screening of his latest film, Maestro. For the daddy-and-daughter outing, Lea looked adorable in a long leopard-print Dolce & Gabbana dress with puff sleeves, which she paired with shiny champagne Mary Jane ballet flats and a black and red box crossbody bag. Meanwhile, Cooper looked striking in a navy blue three-piece suit composed of a blazer, a vest, and pleated trousers. He finished off his look with a black tie and black dress shoes.

Getty Images

Getty Images

Lea even got to meet Cooper’s A Star Is Born costar, Lady Gaga, who showed up to the screening in a sequin-embellished suit from Alexander McQueen.

Charley Gallay//Getty Images

Shayk, who split from Cooper in 2019, opened up about raising Lea in an interview for Harper’s Bazaar’s May 2023 cover story.

“My daughter is a big part of why I started to love myself even more,” Shayk said. “She’ll say, ‘I love Daddy so much, I love Mama so much, I love Nana, I love Babulya—’ who is my mom. Then she goes, ‘And I really love myself.’ And I’m looking at my daughter and I’m like, ‘Wow, I wasn’t like that at six years old.’”

She continued, “We’re teaching our daughter that the most important thing is to be kind to people. Every time we send her to school, we’re like, ‘Just remember kindness and love.’”
