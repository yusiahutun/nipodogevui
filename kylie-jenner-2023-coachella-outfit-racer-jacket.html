Kylie Jenner Subverts Coachella Trends in a Leather Racer Jacket

Coachella might evoke images of flower crowns, fish net cover-ups, and billowing skirts, but Kylie Jenner forged her own sartorial path at the desert music festival.

While attending Weekend 1 of the festival, the Kylie Cosmetics mogul ditched the typical bohemian and Western trends for a look that better fit her own edgy, personal sense of style. Sharing photos of her outfit with fans on Instagram, the mom-of-two looked effortlessly cool in an ivory leather racer jacket from Courrèges. She layered the unique outerwear over a white mesh top with a lace bustier, which was rolled up to reveal her midriff. She finished off the look with a low-rise jean midi skirt with a high center leg slit, white rectangular sunglasses, chunky white Nike x Comme des Garçons, and multiple chunky silver cuff earrings.

In the same Instagram post, Kylie also shared snaps of big sister Kendall Jenner, who joined her during the festival. Kendall opted for a more casual look, wearing a white racerback tank top, denim cut-offs cinched at the waist with a black belt, and a red shoulder bag.

By now, fans are well acquainted with Kylie's penchant for showing off her style via Instagram.

In another recent post, the reality TV star looked glamorous in an abstract take on the little black dress, pulled from Acne Studios's fall 2023 collection. The deconstructed knit piece featured open slits, distressed cutouts, floral embellishments, a high neckline, and a long thin neck piece that doubled as a choker.
