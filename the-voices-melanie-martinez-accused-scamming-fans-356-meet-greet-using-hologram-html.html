The Voice's Melanie Martinez accused of 'scamming' fans with $356 'meet-and-greet' using hologram

Melanie Martinez has been criticized on social media for charging $356 for a VIP meet-and-greet using AI technology.

The 28-year-old singer is currently in the middle of her Portals tour and performed at the Skyla Credit Union Amphitheatre in North Carolina last weekend. 

Some fans paid $356 for VIP packages including a meet-and-greet, but didn't actually come face-to-face with Melanie.

The description of the package on Ticketmaster promised fans an 'interactive portals photo experience' for VIP ticketholders, perhaps leading some fans to believe this meant an in-person meet-and-greet with the singer herself. 

However, various videos shared on TikTok, showed excited fans standing in front of a pink billboard before being presented with an AI-generated hologram of Melanie.

Not real: Melanie Martinez has been criticized for charging $400 for a VIP meet-and-greet using AI technology of herself

Hardcore fans: Concert-goers were told to stand in front of a pink board before the hologram of Melanie appeared

The Dollhouse hitmaker was dressed in a khaki green shredded dress with a flesh-colored top and matching stockings.

Keeping with the theme of her newest album, Melanie completed her look with a face of prosthetics that featured four big eyes, pursed lips, and oversized ears.

As the hologram of Melanie walked into shot, she waved and then turned to face the camera and posed with her arms by her side.

She then curtseyed before waving at the fan again and then blew a kiss before quickly walking off.

Many TikTok users blasted Melanie for the stunt as one person fumed: 'That’s actually insane she would be that lazy to not just show up but has the nerve to charge that.'

Another person moaned: 'That much for a video recording is CRAZZY,' while a third added: 'This should’ve been the FREE alternative for those who couldn’t attend an actual meet and greet LMFAO.'

However, one fan named Kate was quick to defend Melanie and captioned her TikTok video: 'I thought it was cute everyones hating it never said anywhere there was going to be a meet and greet. The concert was so fun #portalstour @Melanie Martinez (sic).'

Speaking to DailyMail.com about the experience, Kate further explained: 'It was never listed that there would be a meet and greet.

'The only people who meet Melanie before concerts are a few people who won contests (usually radio contests) for each show.'

Kate confirmed that she paid $355.90 for her ticket, which also included a VIP-exclusive merch pack and a commemorative VIP laminate.

'I will say, however, nobody has seemed to receive the "Exclusive Portals VIP Digital collectable" that was listed and no one's really sure what it is,' she continued.

'Also I noticed there is a mouse cursor on the over lay of my photo with "Melanie", which I thought was because they were running late to set up at my venue, but came to realize it's on everyone's. Even at different venues which seems like they didn't really seem to care too much about this part sadly.'

'But the concert itself was an amazing experience from the backup dancers to the stage effects. She nailed the performance,' Kate added.

However, many people were still stunned at the price of the ticket as one person said on TikTok: 'pleaseee dont tell me this was expensive,' while another wrote: '$400 for a damn photo booth.'

Someone else claimed: 'This should be illegal to charge for,' to which Kate replied: 'close up tickets to concerts and events are literally hundreds. this was 350 with some merch and a some cute photo ops. I don't see what's so crazy.

'it's not a meet and greet and she never said it was why are you complaining when you didn't even do it?' she added.

Melanie has built up a very strong fanbase ever since she found fame on the third season of The Voice back in 2012. 

However she found herself dragged into a bitter scandal in 2017, when she was accused of raping her former best friend Timothy Heller -  allegations which she strongly denied.

Aspiring musician Timothy claimed that Melanie raped her with a sex toy at a sleepover.

In lengthy posts shared on Twitter in December 2017, she said that although she allowed Melanie to touch her arm as they lay in bed together, it escalated into sexual assault.

A couple of hours after Timothy's accusations, Melanie responded and said that her best friend 'did not say no' to anything they chose to do together. 

'I am horrified and saddened by the statements and story told by Timothy Heller,' Melanie said in her own statement.

'What she and I shared was a close friendship. We tried to help each other. We both had pain in dealing with our individual demons.

'She never said no to what we chose to do together. And although we parted ways, I am sending her love and light always.'
