What happened to Terence Trent D'Arby?

Trent D'Arby was compared to Michael Jackson and Prince at the height of his fame in the 1980s. Unfortunately, he became a recluse after nearly a decade in showbiz, dropped his name and embarked on a new route away from the limelight.

He changed his name to Sananda Francesco Maitreya, a name inspired by his Buddhism. While speaking on Good Morning Britain, he talked of the risk of death if he didn't choose a new path. He told Piers Morgan and Susanna Reid that he had escaped the madness of his past life.

Part of his explanation read as follows:

You have to understand that almost all of my close peers, almost all of them, are dead. Completely buried under the ground or wherever the hell they are on Jupiter, wherever they are right now... I would not have survived that life. It's folly for me to pretend that I would have survived that life. I know that I would not have survived that life.
