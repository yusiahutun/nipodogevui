Suriya 44: Suriya joins hands with director Karthik Subbaraj for his next; calls it 'Love Laughter W

Suriya is undoubtedly one of the most celebrated actors currently working in the industry. His commendable performances and sheer dedication to the projects have always garnered immense appreciation from his die-hard admirers. 

The actor who is currently gearing up for his power-packed high-octane thriller Kanguva is busy with its post-production schedule. Meanwhile, Suriya has now announced his new project with star director Karthik Subbaraj.

Suriya join forces with Karthik Subbaraj for Suriya44

On March 28, Suriya took to his social platform X and shared the news along with a riveting announcement poster and wrote, “New beginnings..! Need all your good wishes! #LoveLaughterWar @karthiksubburaj.” 

Karthik Subbaraj also took to his X account and shared the news about their epic collaboration. He wrote, “My Next Film is with the Ever-Awesome @Suriya_offl sir So Pumped up for this #Suriya44 #LoveLaughterWar #AKarthikSubbarajPadam.” Soon after the news of the collaboration went viral fans took to the comments section and expressed their happiness for their epic team-up. 

A fan wrote, “Unexpected sir, best wishes,” while the other one wrote,” The most unexpected combo announcement ever on Tamil cinema.” The film will be bankrolled by Rajsekhar Pandian & Kaarthekeyen Santhanam under the banner of 2D Entertainments and Stone Bench Films in a joint venture.

Suriya upcoming films

Suriya is currently gearing up for his upcoming thriller Kanguva, helmed by director Siva. The film is touted to be a period drama thriller featuring Suriya in the role of a valiant warrior. Bobby Deol and Disha Patani are making their Tamil debuts with the flick, which also stars Natarajan Subramaniam, Jagapathi Babu, Yogi Babu, Redin Kingsley, Kovai Sarala, Anandaraj, Ravi Raghavendra, KS Ravikumar, and many more. 

Kanguva was produced by K. E. Gnanavel Raja, V. Vamsi Krishna Reddy, and Pramod Uppalapati under the labels Studio Green and UV Creations. The action thriller is slated to release in the middle of 2024.

Karthik Subbaraj's upcoming films

Karthik Subbaraj recently helmed the highly acclaimed 2023 film titled Jigarthanda DoubleX featuring S J Suryah and Raghava Lawrence in the lead roles. The action drama garnered immense praise from audiences and critics for its theme along with outstanding performances by the ensemble cast. 

Apart from Suriya44, Karthik has not yet announced anything related to directorial plans. Meanwhile, he'll be directing Chiyaan Vikram in the upcoming sequel of Mahaan which features Dhruv Vikram as well.
