Canadian actor Kavan Smith is famous for his versatile film and television roles since his debut from the TV series, Destiny Ridge, in 1993. 

He is best known for his roles as Major Evan Lorne in the television show, Stargate Atlantis (2005-2009), and Leland Coulter in the TV series, When Calls the Heart (2015-2020). 

Read on to know more about the actor's personal and professional life. 

Is Kavan Smith Married?

Kavan Smith is someone who has kept his personal life extremely private. 

According to Country Living, Kavan got married in a small wedding several years ago. But no other information about his wedding or wife has ever surfaced.

Despite all that secrecy, in 2011, Kavan did teet about how he met his wife,

I met my wife, Calamity while doing volunteer work at a treatment center.

Well, from that tweet, it's apparent that Kavan is, married. Further, in 2017, he tweeted that he could not share his wedding pictures because his wife had refrained from doing so.

Apart from those occasional tweets about his wife, Kavan has also tweeted about his kids.

Age, Family

Kavan was born as Kavan Joel Smith on 6th May 1970 in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada.

His parents got divorced when he was a young child, and after that, Kavan raised by his single father, alongside a brother.

In his childhood days, Kavan was a sporty kid, focused on martial arts and football. But in his mid-teens, he cultivated a love for performing.

His Career

Kavan's career started right after his graduation from Mount Royal University. He landed the lead role on the Canadian TV show, Destiny Ridge (1993).

After that, he slowly established himself as a versatile actor with roles in TV shows such as The Outer Limits (1995-2001), The 4400 (2005-2007), Eureka (2010-2012), Rogue (2013-2014), Human Target (2010), and Supernatural (2008-2015) to name a few.

Moreover, his film credits include films like Fearless (1999), Mission to Mars (2000), See Spot Run (2001), The Cult (2010), Hello, It's Me (2015), and Love on the Menu (2019).

Apart from his acting credentials, Kavan also added writing credits on his name by writing the story for his movie Love on the Menu.
