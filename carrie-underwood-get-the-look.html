Carrie Underwood Shows You How to Get Ready for Spring in Skinny Jeans, a Fancy Sweater + More G

Carrie Underwood is a fashionista who likes to experiment with her looks on stage and off. But when you catch her going casual in jeans, she still takes things up a notch with her styling and accessories. This spring-ready look is easy to recreate on the cheap, without sacrificing any of the chic!

The look is built around skinny jeans in a medium wash, like these Aeropostale skinnies you can grab at the mall. They're usually on sale for less than $20, so consider that a serious steal.

The singer's embellished beige top has volume, which pairs nicely with the slim bottoms -- it's all about balance with this look. Dorothy Perkins has a similar version for just $27, which will work for your wardrobe across several seasons. You can don it in the fall and the spring, and that makes it an investment item in your closet. Win-win!

The priciest pieces in the look are accessories, but the good news is both are classic and can be worn with lots of other outfits, meaning the cost-per-wear cancels out the cash you shell out upfront.

This Legacy Leather Wristlet by Coach, always a popular choice for affordable yet upscale accessories, comes in at $109, but it'll never go out of style. You can also pick whatever color you wish.

These caged Steve Madden heels are $150, but they are a summer staple and like the wristlet, the cost-per-wear makes them worthwhile. They are about as close to Carrie's pair as you are going to get, too.

There you have it. That's how you ape Carrie Underwood's street style for under $300.
