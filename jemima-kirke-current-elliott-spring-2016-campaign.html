Jemima Kirke Stars in Current/Elliott's Spring 2016 Ad Campaign

We may still be braving through the cold of winter, but spring has definitely sprung in the world of fashion. Campaigns for the new season have already begun to roll out, and there are plenty of famous faces (like Jaden Smith for Louis Vuitton and Karlie Kloss for Topshop) amidst the bunch.

One of our favorites so far? JemimaKirke's ads for vintage-inspired brand Current/Elliott, which feature the blonde beauty at her absolute best. In the series of photos, the Girls star dons a series of denim-based ensembles, from a button-up midi skirt to majorly flared jeans and destroyed boyfriend skinnies. Kirke was photographed by Cass Bird and styled by Stevie Dance for the shoot, and she looks simply gorgeous.
