Premier League hair transplants before and after including Gabriel's taxi advert

Hair loss is a pretty big concern for men prone to baldness and a number of wiry-haired Premier League stars have gone under the knife to get the haircut of their dreams

Arsenal defender Gabriel Magalhaes went viral this week after he underwent a hair transplant - though he's far from the first Premier League star to do so.

The Brazilian was outed as the face of a London-based hair transplant clinic after his freshly-seeded head was spotted on the outside of a black cab, much to the amusement of footy fans.

But his hairy high jinks are anything but unusual. Dozens of elite-level stars in English football have had similar work done, including one of Gabriel's former team-mates.

So without further ado, here are eight Premier League stars who have had hair transplants and what they looked like afterwards.
