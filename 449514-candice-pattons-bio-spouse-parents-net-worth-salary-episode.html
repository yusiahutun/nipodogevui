Candice Patton's biography

Candice was born on June 24th, 1988, in Jackson, Mississippi, United States. She was born to an African-American mother, Ariana Jackson, and a white father, Paul Patton. Her mother was an opera artist, while her father is an engineer.

Candice's parents divorced when she was five years old but maintained a good relationship for the sake of their children. She has two siblings called Andrian and Kaitly Patton. They spent most of their early life with their mother but occasionally visited their father.

Miss Patton attended Plano East Senior High School before joining the Southern Methodist University. She graduated with a degree in Fine Arts, majoring in Theatre.
