The Perfect One-Day Itinerary in Downtown Laurel Laurel Mercantile

If you’re looking to experience the charm of a small, southern town in one day, we have the perfect one in mind. Laurel, Mississippi has plenty of cultural, tasty and fun places to shop and see. While there is an extensive list of things to do over a 2-3 day period, a day in Laurel is the perfect amount of time to get the Home Town experience. Here’s a list of things that a Laurel local would recommend!
