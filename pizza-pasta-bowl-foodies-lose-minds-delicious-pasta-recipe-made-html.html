Pizza pasta bowl: Foodies lose their minds over 'the most delicious pasta recipe ever made'

A home cook has shared a unique 'pizza pasta bowl' recipe that foodies all over are praising for being extremely 'delicious' and simple to make.

Sophia, 24, from London, is best known for sharing recipes and homemaking content on social media.

The British woman has just wowed millions by revealing a soon-to-be-favourite dinner idea to her fans.

The pizza pasta bowl requires garlic, onion, pepperoni, tomato-based pasta sauce, mixed herbs, grated mozzarella, and grated cheddar.

Sophia's one pan recipe can be made in under 20 minutes excluding pasta-prep time.

After first boiling some large spiral pasta and setting it aside to cool, Sophia started on the delicious sauce. 

The amateur chef began by frying chopped garlic and onion in olive oil until the onions softened and browned.

She then mixed in a generous amount of tomato-based pasta sauce.

For the next step, Sophia added in grated mozzarella and cheddar and stirred the mixture with Italian herbs until the cheese had melted into the sauce.

Sophia added the pasta in and mixed it with the sauce, she then covered the pan in a layer of mozzarella cheese akin to a pizza.

The last step was placing chopped pepperoni on top of the dish and then putting a lid on while the top layer of cheese melts.

How to make a pizza pasta bowl 

Ingredients:

Olive oil

3 cloves garlic

1 small onion

Grated mozzarella to taste

Grated parmesan to taste

Mixed herbs

Tomato-based pasta sauce to taste

Pepperoni 

Method: 

  • Boil pasta and set aside
  • Fry chopped garlic and onion in olive oil until onion softens and browns
  • Pour in tomato pasta sauce and mix well
  • Sprinkle mixed herbs along with salt and pepper 
  • Add in grated mozzarella and parmesan and stir into sauce 
  • Cover pan in a layer of mozzarella, like a pizza
  • Place sliced pepperoni on top of cheese layer
  • Cover the pan with a lid and wait for the cheese to melt
  • Enjoy!
