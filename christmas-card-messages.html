126 Best Christmas Card Messages to Write in Christmas Card 2023

In an age where email and texts hit your inbox nonstop and your snail mail is mostly junk mail, there's something extra special about sending and receiving a Christmas card in the mail, but what should you write in it? Here we give you 126 ideas for Christmas card messages to answer that question.

Scroll through these short sayings to find the one that feels just right to write to your friends and family this year in your Christmas greetings. Some are heartfelt and inspirational, while others are lighthearted or funny. We also have messages customized for any recipient you can think of: friends, family, coworkers, bosses, teachers, clients, neighbors—even in-laws and boyfriends and girlfriends. All of the messages also double as great Christmas Instagram captions too, or you can look to Christmas puns, Santa jokes, or Christmas quotes to send Merry Christmas wishes to your loved ones.

The handwritten addition of one of these greetings will personalize any store-bought printed Christmas card and is sure to bring a smile as soon as it's opened.

Now time for those Christmas card messages so you can get to writing and addressing those envelopes (perhaps with your favorite Christmas songs or Christmas movies playing in backgrounds to keep things festive). 'Tis the season!

Short Christmas Card Messages

Westend61//Getty Images

It doesn't take many words to express a heartfelt greeting.

  • Have yourself a merry little Christmas.
  • Wishing you peace, joy, and love this Christmas and always.
  • May this season be full of light and laughter.
  • Cheers to eggnog by the fire, warm holiday memories, and you.
  • May the spirit of the holidays fill your home with love and peace.
  • Wishing you and yours a merry Christmas and prosperity in the New Year.

Christmas Card Messages for Friend

Dan Brownsword//Getty Images

Whether they're your bestie or a more casual buddy, let 'em feel the love this Christmas.

  • Feeling so grateful this Christmas for having such an amazing friend.
  • Thanks for being such a good friend! You are one of the best parts of my life and have wonderful Christmas!
  • Not even Santa could bring a better friend. Merry Christmas, bestie!
  • The gift of your friendship is my favorite Christmas present.
  • When I count my Christmas blessings, you're at the top of the list!
  • Every Christmas is merrier because you're a part of it, my forever friend.
  • The holidays are a lot more special when celebrating with great friends like you.
  • We may live far apart, but I celebrate this season with you in my heart. Merry Christmas, dear friend.

Christmas Card Messages for Your Boss

JGI/Jamie Grill//Getty Images

When writing a Christmas card to your boss, keep it warm and professional.

  • Thank you for your leadership and dedication this year. Best wishes this holiday season!
  • Thank you for inspiring me to do my best every day. Merry Christmas!
  • Wishing a wonderful holiday to a wonderful mentor.
  • Thanks for being an outstanding leader and have a wonderful holiday season!
  • Our team wouldn't be the same without you. Merry Christmas!
  • Thank you for another outstanding year! May you have the Merriest Christmas!
  • Happy holidays to a terrific mentor—looking forward to doing great work together in the New Year.
  • Let the spirit of Christmas warm your home and fill your heart with joy.
  • May you share joyful memories, laughter, and good cheer with your loved ones this Christmas.
