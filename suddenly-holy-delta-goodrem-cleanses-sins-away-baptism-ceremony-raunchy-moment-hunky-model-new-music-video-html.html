Delta Goodrem cleanses her sins away in baptism ceremony in new music video

She was seen getting hot and heavy with a mystery man in snippets of her latest music video on Thursday.

But in newly released images from the same video, Delta Goodrem, 31, appears to have had a religious awakening - with the songstress taking part in what looks like a baptism ceremony.

Covered up and dressed in all white, the Sydney-born artist looks demure and focused as she stands in water in the video for her latest single The River.

According to Delta, The River, an up-tempo catchy guitar driven track, is about redemption from a bad relationship.

'The River was written as a story, the songs theme is one of redemption, about cleansing off volatile relationships and washing off that energy and starting a new,' she said.

'It has a really fun sing along vibe and is a great song to perform live – I can't wait to get out there and perform it on the tour!

The multi-platinum artist has sold 8million records worldwide with four No. 1 albums, nine No.1 singles, 17 top 10 hits, 14 ARIA Awards, a Silver Logie Award and three World Music Awards.
