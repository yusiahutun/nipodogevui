What Is the Net Worth of Alison Sweeney? Four Fu Facts About the Actress

Based on her extensive career in the movie industry, the net worth of actress Alison Sweeney is over a million dollars region.

American actress Alison Sweeney who fans also know as Sami Brady from “Days of Our Lives,” is an established opera persona. The actress has made her name by starring in daytime television and made-for-TV films.

The California native who started her entertainment career as a five-year-old has become an established star in Hollywood. Sweeney’s movie career began in 1984 when she starred in an episode of the series “Simon and Simon” by CBS. 

Afterward, she made her big screen debut in the 1990 semi-autobiographical movie “The End of Innocence.” Sweeney then followed up that performance with appearances in “Friends” and “Las Vegas.”

Sweeney also played Hannah Swensen in the “Murder She Baked” telefilm series. In addition, she starred in “A Plum Pudding Murder Mystery,” “A Peach Cobbler Mystery,” “A Deadly Recipe,” and “Just Desserts.”

The actress landed a significant role in the ABC sitcom “Family Man” and “Brand New Life.” Nowadays, the actress has become famous for starring in several Hallmark movies.  Some of her Hallmark movie credits include “Love on the Air,” “The Irresistible Blueberry Farm,” and “Christmas at Holly Lodge.” Others include “Recovered,” “The Wrong Man,” and “Vines That Bind.”

She has hosted shows occasionally. From 2006 to 2016, the actress hosted NBC’s competition reality show “The Biggest Loser.” Sweeney has also been doing a bit of directing. 

Alison Sweeney’s Net Worth

Sweeney has become a recognized and bankable star with more than a decade of experience working in Hollywood. Apart from her movie earnings, the actress has also hosted several shows. 

For her role as the host of “The Biggest Loser” from 2006 until 2016, Sweeney got paid $45,000 per episode. Her Hallmark and Disney movies have also earned the actress considerable money. According to Celebrity Net Worth, as of 2022, the net worth of Alison Sweeney is $9 million. Sweeney earned this from her movie, hosting gig, and book sales. 

Sweeney Lives a Frugal Lifestyle

Although her net worth is in the millions, Sweeney does not spend as much as you would expect from a Hollywood star. The actress is reportedly concerned with ensuring her fortune lasts and not spending lavishly. 

Sweeney lives her life on a budget, and although she does invest occasionally, she is concerned with saving money for her family. This is in line with some celebrities who also like frugality. 

Also, her cutthroat schedule of being on movie sets has reduced her spending habits. She spends most of her time thinking of how to perfect a role rather than what to spend her money on. 

Sweeney Is Also an Author

Away from her busy schedule acting and sometimes directing films, Sweeney also finds the time to write books. The actress is also an accomplished author with several successful books. 

Some of her novels include ‘The Star Attraction,” “Opportunity Knocks,” and “Scared Scriptless.” Sweeney also has written two non-fiction books, memoirs, “All The Days Of My Life (So Far),” and “The Mommy Diet.”

Speaking about her venturing into writing, Sweeney initially stated that it was scary because the process was new to her. In addition, unlike acting or producing films, the actress felt intimidated by attempting to write a book. 

However, her decision to write a book stems from her passion as a storyteller. She could write stories based on her idea and concepts, which felt satisfying. 

Sweeney’s Weight Struggles 

For several years as an actress, Sweeney struggled with inconsistent weight issues. Sweeney tried fad diets, but that didn’t help keep her weight in check. Eventually, she discovered that her weight issues also affected her self-esteem. The actress noted: 

“From the time I was 12, I was overweight, and as a teen, I definitely struggled with my weight.”

Sweeney tried fad diets, but that didn’t help keep her weight in check. Eventually, she discovered that her weight issues also affected her self-esteem. The actress was felt insecure and unconfident. This actress has since adopted the mindset of loving her body and working towards a healthier lifestyle.

She is conscious of what she eats and gets plenty of exercises. Sweeney goes for runs, hiking, circuit training, and yoga. Eating fruits and vegetables also reduced her sugar intake and carb cravings. The actress joins the likes of Jessica Simpson, who also struggled with weight issue.

Sweeney Has Been Married for 22 Years

Alison Sweeney married her husband, David Sanov, on July 8, 2000, after dating for three years. The couple has since become parents to two kids; son Benjamin and daughter Megan. Speaking on how she met Sanov, who is now a retired California Highway Patrol Officer, Sweeney said: 

“Our parents are friends. His dad and my mom played the violin for feature film soundtracks, so we’ve actually known each other our whole lives. I totally had a crush on him when we were kids.” 

However, with a busy career that has her spending more time away from her family, Sweeney explained that she works hard to make her marriage work. According to her, she and her husband work hard to keep their relationship solid apart from their parenting roles. 

They often try to spend time together, just the two of them. However, during this yearly wedding anniversary, the couple also evaluates their marriage, noting specific individual things to work on.
