Paris Hilton hailed a 'cool mom' after pushing baby boy in $4,900 Dior stroller

Paris Hilton has taken to her Instagram to share rare ‘photo dump’ pictures with her son Pheonix, although all eyes were on his Dior stroller.

Paris Hilton‘s first child, Pheonix Barron Hilton Reum was born via surrogacy earlier this year, with her partner, Carter Reum. Announcing the birth of her child on Instagram back in January, the star shared photos of herself holding hands with her newborn son, simply captioned: “You are already loved beyond words.”

Paris Hilton pushes pricey Dior stroller

Taking to her 24.1 million Instagram followers, celebrity Paris Hilton shared a rare ‘photo dump’ with her son Pheonix, although his face was not fully visible in the snaps.

Instead, the main attraction of the slideshow was his Dior stroller, crafted from water-repellent canvas, which comes in at a costly $4,900.

Paris opted for a casual outfit, pairing a graphic T-Shirt with flared leopard print trousers. She then topped the look off with a New Era cap and a pair of sunglasses.

Fans call Paris a ‘cool mom’

Of course, fans rushed to Paris’ post, and of course, the Dior stroller.

“Why wouldn’t she have a Dior stroller 😂,” laughed one fan.

“Just when you thought Paris couldn’t impress us even more, she pulls out her Dior stroller. Sooo cuteeeee,” commented another.

Demi Lovato wrote: “Omg you’re SO CUTE.”

“She’s not like a regular mom. She’s a cool mom,” referenced another.

The star kept her son’s birth private

After the birth of their son, the couple revealed they kept the birth of their child a secret, even from their closest friends and family.

The star made the shocking revelation that even her mother wasn’t aware of his birth until he was a week old.

Speaking about the secrecy on her podcast, This is Paris, the 42-year-old said:

“I just feel that my life has been so public, and I’ve never really had anything just be mine,” she said. “I feel like just my life in so many ways has been invaded. I just really felt that I wanted to have this journey be us only.”

She revealed that, of course, keeping the huge secret from everyone was extremely hard, although it meant the news wasn’t leaked before the couple shared it themselves.
