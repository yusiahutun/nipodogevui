Harry Potter star Rupert Grint's girlfriend Georgia Groome gives birth to baby girl

Harry Potter star Rupert Grint and his girlfriend Georgia Groome are proud parents to a baby girl.

The couple, who have been together for nine years, confirmed the birth in a statement released earlier this evening.

A statement from the couple's publicist said: "Rupert Grint and Georgia Groome are delighted to confirm the birth of their baby girl.

"We would please ask that you respect their privacy at this very special time."

Rupert, 31, and Georgia, 28, announced they were expecting their first child together last month, requesting privacy from their fans.

A statement read: "Rupert Grint and Georgia Groome are excited to announce they are expecting a baby and would please ask for privacy at this time."

The couple were later seen shopping for supplies in London during the lockdown, Georgia's baby bump on show as they strolled through the streets.

Georgia, who rose to fame with a starring role in the hit teen flick Angus, Thongs and Perfect Snogging, reportedly started dating Rupert back in 2011.
