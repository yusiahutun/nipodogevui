Naked Attraction star breaks down in tears as she slams date for having 'small balls'

A star of Naked Attraction slammed her date for having "small balls".

The Channel 4 show returned with a bang on Wednesday evening (March 6) and saw Illy on the hunt for a new man. The 33-year-old model appeared on the show back in 2021 but failed to form a sexual connection with her date Iain.

Illy, who hails from Mansfield, whittled it down to two men before choosing Tom. Straight to the point, Illy said: "I picked Tom because he's fit as f**k. Straight 10."

After stopping at a few other places, the reality stars decided to call it a night and headed back to the hotel. Illy hooked a 'do not disturb' sign on their door handle, and fans can only assume what happened next.

Two weeks later, the duo returned and discussed how things had developed since their date. "The date was absolutely fantastic," gushed Illy. "I had a proper cracking time."

She went on to reveal that they stayed an extra night because they were enjoying each other's company so much. But their romance came to an end just days later.

"I think it was about four days later I got hit with the friend zone," Illy explained. She proceeded to read out the text she received from Tom.

The TV star began: "I'd just like to say I'm sorry for how I've been these last few days. I've been working stupid hours. I've realised I've not got time for anything."

Illy threw her phone as she read out the final part of Tom's message which said: "I'm really sorry but I wish you all the best." The model then broke down in tears as she fumed: "I feel f***ing stupid that I let my guard down and let myself be vulnerable."

Things took an award turn when Tom joined Illy on the sofa. "So I'm not your type then? Why didn't you say that?" Illy fumed. "If you wanted to make time for it to work then you would've done, but your balls aren't big enough to say that." Illy confirmed she wouldn't give up on looking for love before walking away from Tom.
