Ana Golja is a famous actress and singer from Canada. Ana Golja is widely known for portraying Ariana Berlin in the 2015 TV movie Full Out and Zoë Rivas in the teen dramas Degrassi: Next Class (2016) and Degrassi: The Next Generation.

Career and Professional Life

Talking about her career, Golja made her acting debut in the Canadian police drama 1-800-Missing in 2005 as Lucy. Before joining the regular cast of Connor Undercover in 2010, she had minor roles in the TV shows Flashpoint and How to Be Indie.

She portrayed Liz in Clue in 2011. She played her first significant role as a member of the What’s Up Warthogs! the main cast in 2010. Before obtaining the position of Zo Rivas in the Canadian teen drama Degrassi: Next Class in 2013, she took part in small projects and a web series.

Golja was nominated for a Canadian Screen Award for her performance as Ariana Berlin in the 2015 television film Full Out: The Ariana Berlin Movie.

Roy “Royalty” Hamilton, who produced the Full Out soundtrack, had Golja record the song “Feel So Good.” Golja was working on her debut EP as of January 2016. Over several weeks in November and December 2017, she released the EP with the working title Epilogue.

From December 20 to December 22, 2018, Golja co-hosted the Albanian festival Festivali I Kings 57, which serves as the nation’s annual selection for the Eurovision Song Contest.
