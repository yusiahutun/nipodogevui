10 Best Outdoor Fans That'll Cool You Down in 2021

Mosquitoes are a notorious warm-weather pest, but there’s a super simple way to stop them from ruining your backyard fun: Buy one of the best outdoor fans to keep bugs away! Beyond creating a mosquito-free fortress, the best outdoor fans will also cool you down in the summer heat. That’s their intended purpose, after all. 

This surprisingly low-tech solution works wonders because mosquitoes are weak fliers. All it takes is a gentle breeze from a medium-sized rotating fan to deter these winged bloodsuckers. The gusts of wind from the fan will also disperse the scent of carbon dioxide and other chemical cues that mosquitoes use to find their next meal. 

This is not to say you should skip the insect-repellant spray, electric bug zappers, and citronella candles — a combination of these methods makes the most sense. Also, the fan technique works best if you only need a small circle of protection. If you’re hosting a backyard barbecue for the neighborhood, you’re probably better off with traditional bug spray.

While there are more permanent solutions for getting rid of mosquitoes — like draining any stagnant bodies of water — an inexpensive outdoor fan is a great quick fix. We’ve rounded up some of our favorites, so you can get back to more important things, like sipping lemonade on your outdoor rocking chair.
