Hong Kong singer Keung To more handsome than BTS, Chris Hemsworth, Pedro Pascal? Mirror star voted t

Posted on YouTube, the list has been published annually by English writer TC Candler and The Independent Critics since 1990, and 2,656 had voted in this year’s exercise via monetisation platform Patreon.

Many were quick to congratulate Keung To in the YouTube comments section, saying they were proud of him – bar one who thought the poll was rigged. Whether a Mirror fan or not, it is great to see so many Asian faces have made it to both The 100 Most Handsome Faces and The 100 Most Beautiful Faces lists.

V (BTS), Bang Chan (Stray Kids), Chinese actor Zhang Zhehan, Jisoo (Blackpink) and Thai singer Sitala all made it to the top 10 on the two lists.

Here are the top five in both rankings.

Most Handsome Faces

5. Lucien Laviscount (actor)

4. Ni-ki (singer/member of K-pop boy band Enhypen)

3. Keung To (singer/actor)

2. Henry Cavill (actor)

1. Timothée Chalamet (actor)

Most Beautiful Faces

5. Jasmine Tookes (model)

3. Minatozaki Sana (singer, member of K-pop girl group Twice)

2. Dasha Taran (influencer)
