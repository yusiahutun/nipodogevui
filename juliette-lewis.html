Juliette Lewis - Bio, Age, Height, Career, Net Worth, Facts

Juliette Lewis is an American actress and singer. Juliette Lewis is well known for appearing in off-beat characters in movies and TV shows including ‘Natural Born Killers,’  ‘Wayward Pines,’ ‘Starsky and Hutch,’ ‘Hysterical Blindness,’ and ‘Old School.’ Likewise, in the early 1990s, she became an ‘it girl’ of American cinema.

Early Life

Juliette Lewis was born on 21 June 1973 in Los Angeles, California. She is 50 years old. She was born to her father Geoffrey Lewis and her mother Glenis Duggan. Likewise, her father was an actor and her mother was a graphic designer.

Career

In 1987, Juliette appeared in the television movie ‘Home Fires,’ and also played the TV show I Married Dora in the same year. In 1988, she played in a couple of episodes of the television show ‘Facts of Life’ and in the movie ‘My Stepmother Is an Alien.’

Likewise, she appeared in the movie National Lampoon’s Christmas Vacation in 1989 and the drama ‘Crooked Hearts’ in 1991. Lewis Starred with Brad Pitt in the TV movie ‘Too Young to Die?’ in 1990.

In 1992, she starred with Woody Allen in the movie Husbands and Wives. After that, she played a role in many blockbusters and critically acclaimed projects Kalifornia (1993), What’s Eating Gilbert Grape (1993), and Natural Born Killers (1994).

In 1995, Juliette Lewis appeared with DiCaprio in the drama ‘Basketball Diaries’ and Kathryn Bigelow’s movie ‘Strange Days.’ Later that year, she played alongside George Clooney in the film ‘From Dusk Til Dawn.’

Similarly, she appeared in the comedy movies ‘Some Girl’ in 1998, ‘The Other Sister’ and ‘The 4th Floor’ in 1999. Then, she played in the thriller ‘The Way of the Gun’ in 2001 and the crime film ‘Picture Claire’ in 2002.

Also, in the 2000s, she played guest roles on TV shows including ‘Free for All,’ ‘Dharma and Greg,’ ‘Pancake Mountain,’ and ‘My Name is Earl.’
