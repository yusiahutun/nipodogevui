Kim Kardashian Wears Metallic Bra and Lace Shorts

Brian Prahl//Splash News

Some provocative highlights of Kim Kardashian's personal style have included exposed lingerie, near-naked lace panels, biker shorts and, of course, a skin tight silhouette. But earlier this week, she wore all of the above in one outfit.

The reality star was spotted arriving at a friend's Hollywood home on Tuesday in a baby blue ensemble that turned heads for several reasons—her unbuttoned top revealed a disco-metallic bralette, and for bottoms, she opted for skin-tight bermuda-length bikers with sheer lace panels. Kim finished off the look with strappy barely-there Yeezy sandals.

It's not the dinner look you were probably expecting. For starters, who wears biker shorts out? (And who knew they came in lace?) But on another note, it's a departure from Kim's usual uniform of midi dresses and neutral color palettes. Whether she's going for a full-out style revamp or not, Kardashian just proved her style isn't as predictable as you'd think.
