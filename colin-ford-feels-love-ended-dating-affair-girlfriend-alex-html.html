Colin Ford Feels More Than Love; Ended Dating Affair With Girlfriend Alex?

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: May 07, 2017

These days, social media has been the easiest way for a celebrity or any of us to share our day to day life or our personal stories. When some celebrities are not even active in the social media, there are some of the celebrities who use social networking sites to flaunt their personal and professional life.

The twenty-year-old Young Artist Award winner actor Colin Ford is one of those aspiring artists who has used Instagram to flaunt his new love interest. The handsome and talented actor Colin, who has been rumored to be dating the gorgeous ladies like Isabelle Fuhrman (2012) and Chloë Grace Moretz has broken many hearts of his female fans by posting images with his girlfriend on Instagram.

 Colin’s relationship with Alex

Collin introduced a gorgeous girl named Alex to us in early 2014 by sharing a cute picture of them on his Instagram. On November 4, 2014, he shared an image of him kissing Alex that provided a sure shot hint about their affair.

Later on, Colin revealed that they began dating since August 2014, despite the fact that they graduated from the same college. In the caption, he complimented the gift that Alex gave to him as they celebrated six months together on Feb 6, 2015.

Looking at the pictures that Collin still has on his Instagram, it seems that the cute couple enjoyed a blissful relationship.

It appears that Colin and Alex have already separated, as Colin is in love with a new lady, which is evident through his Instagram.

Colin’s new love interest

Colin, who has never been guessed as a gay, is now in a new relationship. On September 7, 2015, he shared a picture of his mysterious lady love and pet dog with a caption-

“My girls make my Monday's better. How's everyone's Monday going??”

In the picture, his girl is seen wearing a ring on her ring finger which made people wonder about who she is. Recently, on March 13, 2017, he posted a very cute image of him and his mysterious girlfriend with a caption-

“This is what love feels like... or something more ?? Pc: @katebosworth”

By the looks of it, the two may be on a voyage of maintaining the height of relationship goals. Keep going, amigos!

More about Colin Ford

The Joe McAlister from the Under the Dome (2013-2015), Colin Ford was born in Nashville, Tennessee on September 12, 1996, and enjoyed being in front of cameras from a very young age.

As Ford had very supportive parents, they helped him enter the entertainment business. At the age of four, he modeled for print ads, and at the age of five, he made his acting debut with Sweet Home Alabama. Talking about his siblings, he has a younger brother.

Colin has been active in the industry since 2001 and has made his appearance in many television series and films. He is popular for providing his voice in Jake and the Never Land Pirates (2011-2013), Family Guy (2010-2017) and appearing in CSI: Miami (2010), The Mob Doctor (2012-2013), Under the Dome (2013-2015), and Supernatural (2007-2016).
