Joe Mucheru Surprised by Miguna's Claims on Election Rigging: &quot;You Know All this from Canada?&quot;

  • CS Joe Mucheru and Miguna Miguna were embroiled in a war of words on Twitter over the upcoming Supreme court case on the presidential results
  • It started when Miguna alleged Mucheru was involved in some kind of electoral malpractice in the favour of Azimio
  • The CS would have none of that and demanded that the exiled barrister leaves him alone

ICT Cabinet secretary Joe Mucheru has been left puzzled by lawyer Miguna Miguna's claims of election rigging.

This was after the exiled barrister took to his social media platforms to claim that through Mucheru, the Azimio la Umoja One Kenya Coalition party had hacked the IEBC system.

No sooner had Miguna made the claims than the CS questioned how the lawyer was privy to the allegations, yet he was in Canada.

"And you know all this from Canada?" Mucheru responded to Miguna's tweet.

The CS would later ask the self-professed general and his counterpart Ahmednasir Abdullahi to leave him alone and instead concentrate on the upcoming Supreme court battle.

"I think lawyers @MigunaMiguna and @ahmednasirlaw should stick to their profession. Always @mucheru this @mucheru that, don't you have your own lawyers to deal with? It's your big day at the Supreme Court tomorrow, FOCUS! or are you going to be spectators like me?" he wrote.

Miguna insisted more that Mucheru was involved in electoral malpractice but did not provide proof. Their Twitter war thus degenerated into an exchange of accusations and counter-accusations.
