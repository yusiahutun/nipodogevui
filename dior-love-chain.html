Jennifer Lawrence Lingerie Dior Love Chain

What would you do for love? Dior posed this question to their brand ambassador Jennifer Lawrence for a new social campaign called Dior Love Chain, and naturally, the actress's answer is perfectly relatable. Watch below to see what she has to say about love and wearing lingerie.

Lawrence is one of several major celebrities, influencers, artists, and Dior faces in the video, alongside the likes of Rihanna and Charlize Theron. When asked what she would do for love, Lawrence hilariously replies that she would "pretend that sleeping in sexy underwear is comfortable." In another Dior Love Chain video, Bella Hadid says she would do "anything" for love—is she a closet Meatloaf fan?—while Robert Pattinson says he would "try to make himself a better person." Right in the feels.

You can watch all the Dior Love Chain videos on the brand's Instagram—but there's a charity component as well. Share your own answer to "what would you do for love?" on Instagram, Twitter, or Weibo with the hashtag #DiorLoveChain and Dior Parfums, together with the face of Miss Dior Natalie Portman, will donate $1 to the charity We Movement. The charity supports the education of young women in Kenya and worldwide, and so far Dior has collected nearly $14,000 and counting.
