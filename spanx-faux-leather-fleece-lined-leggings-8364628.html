Spanxs Famously Flattering Faux Leather Leggings Are Now Fleece-Lined

I don’t own jeans. While reading those four words may have just caused your jaw to drop to the floor, allow me to explain: I understand how wonderful most people find jeans to be, I’m just not among them. Chalk it up to my refusal to be bound by zippers and buttons — pull-on silhouettes and I just tend to get along better. If I’m not wearing a dress, I’m definitely in a billowy top and pair of leggings. And, since I tend to buy leggings on leggings on leggings, I’m definitely always in search of the next pair ready to become a staple in my wardrobe. 

At the moment, that’s the Spanx Faux Leather Fleece-Lined Leggings. The brand’s famous faux leather leggings are now equipped with a game-changing feature that has me begging for crisp fall temperatures — they’ve been reimagined with cozy fleece lining. The sleek faux leather silhouette features a contoured waistband for smoothing compression that isn’t overly constricting. They’re also free of a center seam, so you don’t have to worry about dreaded camel toe. The leggings are available in three different inseams (regular, petite and tall) and sizes from XS to 3XL. Reviewers say they can run small, so it might be best to size up.

I personally love that they’re available in a petite fit, so it will be possible to find a pair to fit my 5-foot curvy frame. I’m envisioning wearing these fleece-lined leggings everywhere — and with everything — this fall. Cropped sweatshirts, oversized sweater coats, trenches, flowy tunics — the sky’s the limit when it comes to how I’m planning on styling them. 

Fellow InStyle editors have praised Spanx’s faux leather leggings before, and the fleece-lined iteration is already racking up five-star reviews shortly after launching. One shopper said the fleece lining “makes the leggings soft against your skin, without being too warm or bulky.” If you need a little more swaying, additional shoppers raved that the “buttery soft” faux leather is “really flexible while looking structured,” the style is “very comfortable and flattering,” and the leggings “shape without binding.” And there’s this rave reviewer, who simply stated that “the best leggings just got better.”

I’m definitely ready to add yet another pair of leggings to my wardrobe, thanks to all the appealing features of this fleece-lined Spanx pair. If you’re as into leggings as I am, they’re available for $110 at Spanx — grab a pair before they inevitably sell out.
