Lisa Vanderpump and Katie Maloney arrive to the season 11 reunion taping of Vanderpump Rules - a yea

Lisa Vanderpump led the arrivals for the filming of Vanderpump Rules' season 11 reunion special, which took place at a studio in Burbank on Saturday.

The 63-year-old reality television personality stood out as she stepped out of a private vehicle and prepared to begin working on the program's upcoming special episode.

The former member of The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills - who recently opened up about how Scandoval affected her businesses - was also joined by several other members of the show's cast at the taping.

Vanderpump wore a patterned light blue jacket on top of black leggings, which were paired with matching high-heeled shoes. 

The entrepreneur also carried a sizable purse, and her gorgeous brunette hair fell onto her shoulders.

Vanderpump Rules, which serves as a spinoff from The Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills, premiered in 2013.

The show is centered on the professional and personal lives of various individuals working at Vanderpump's various restaurants and bars.

Other individuals featured on the program include Raquel Leviss, Ariana Madix and James Kennedy.
