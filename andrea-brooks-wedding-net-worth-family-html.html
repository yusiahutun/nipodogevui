Andrea Brooks Husband, Family, Net Worth

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: August 13, 2019

Discovered at the age of 15, Andrea Brooks shot to international superstardom by portraying Faith Carter in When Calls The Heart and Eve Teschmacher in Supergirl.

Supernatural, iZombie and UnReal are some of Andrea's notable small-screen performances. Elsewhere on big-screen, she boasts acting credits on What Goes Up, No Men Beyond This Point and 50/50.

The University of British Columbia graduate, Andrea owns a Bachelor's and Masters of Arts degree. As a kid, she recieved formal schooling in Dance, Theater, Music, and Figure Skating.

Engaged To Mystery Man!

A sweetheart, Andrea persisted on keeping a squeaky clean image while ascending to worldwide fame. Staying on-brand with her teenage superstar status, she refrained from sharing intimate aspects of her life.

But then, the Supergirl actress shocked her fanbase by hinting an engagement. When she shared a snap of a ring in July 2018 via Facebook, fans concluded that she might have gotten engaged. No caption accompanied the post.

For her part, Andrea neither shared the details of the proposal nor did she spoke on her mystery boyfriend-turned-fiance.

Wedding, Husband & Married

Barely two months into their lives as an engaged duo, Andrea and her mystery man tied the knot.

Married at the Heritage Hall in Vancouver, Canada in mid-September 2018, the wedding between the "When Calls The Heart" actress and her husband was a low-key affair. Only select few got to the ceremony.

Despite chronicling the big day on her Instagram, the details surrounding the nuptials remain confined to Andrea and the guests. Interestingly, her mystery husband is absent from the wedding photos.

On the day she walked down the aisle, Andrea wore a gorgeous sleeveless gown. Pamela Anseeuw had handled her makeup.

Andrea Brooks and brother Matt Brooks at her wedding circa September 2018 (Photo:- Matt Brooks' Instagram)

The Supernatural actress and her furtive husband's married life only strengthened with the news of them expecting their first child. In June 2019, she took to Instagram and showcased her baby bump.

Set to become a mother for the very first time by the end of 2019, Andrea revealed her firstborn's gender as a girl.

Bio - Age & Net Worth

Now age 30, Andrea was born in Brantford, Ontario, Canda on 3 March 1989. She inherited the passion from figure skating from her biological mother Leslie, who competed in the sports professionally and later became a trainer.
