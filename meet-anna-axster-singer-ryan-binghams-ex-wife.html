Meet Anna Axster, Singer Ryan Binghams Ex-Wife

Anna Axster is a talented film producer, music video director, and writer who gained recognition for her directorial work on the 2015 film “A Country Called Home.” However, she is most famous as the ex-wife of Ryan Bingham, a multifaceted American singer renowned for his artistic flair. Bingham is a songwriter, guitarist, and actor. 

Axster has lived most of her life away from the spotlight. However, she gained fans’ curiosity after her ex-husband announced his relationship with “Yellowstone” co-star Hassie Harrison years after their marriage fell apart.

Who is Anna Axster, and what became of her marriage to Ryan Bingham? Keep reading to find out.

Who Is Anna Axster?

Despite being a prominent figure, Anna Axster has kept most details about her early life private. Her age, birth date, and hometown remain unknown. Hence, there are no details regarding Axster’s upbringing. In addition, the identity of her parents and siblings is also a mystery to the public. However, online reports suggest she grew up in the United States. 

While there are no details regarding her elementary and high school educational achievements, Axster is well-educated. After high school, she attended the London Metropolitan University and received a Bachelor of Arts in Communications and Audio Visual Production. 

Afterward, she attended the London Film School and holds a MA in Filmmaking from the institution. Axster had always been interested in the movie industry as a kid, and she aspired to be a filmmaker. It’s safe to say that she managed to turn that dream into a reality. 

Anna Axster’s Career 

Like her ex-husband, Axster is in the entertainment industry. She is a well-known filmmaker, video director, and writer. Axster started her career as a director and writer with the short film “Souvenir.”

Axter directed Ryan Bingham’s music videos after they got acquainted. She worked on his music videos for “Southside of Heaven” and “Bread and Water” in 2007.

Anna Axster later directed the films “Sweet Sting” and “A Country Called Home,” her self-written movie that launched her career as a producer. The film was released in 2015 and garnered accolades from the film industry. 

Additionally, Axster worked as the executive producer of “Ryan Bingham Live,” which they taped at the Whitewater Amphitheater on August 6, 2016. She also established a record label, Axster Bingham Records, which she co-owns with her ex-husband, Ryan Bingham. In January 2022, she launched a beverage manufacturing enterprise called Lodestar Spirits.

When Did Anna Axster and Ryan Bingham Get Married?

Before her relationship with Bingham, Axster had never been in public romantic relationships. Hence, he was her first known partner. The two met after she got a hold of Bingham’s demo CD and heard one of his songs that resonated with her. 

Wanting to use the track on one of the films she was working on, Axster decided to meet Bingham and ask his permission personally. Immediately they met, they hit it off and soon became inseparable.

After several years of dating, Axster and Bingham decided to take their relationship up a notch after he popped the question. In 2009, the couple held a private ceremony and exchanged vows in front of close friends and relatives. Their marriage was a happy one. 

The now-divorced couple supported each other’s dreams and experienced success in their respective careers. Unfortunately, their marriage didn’t endure the test of time. Their happily ever after came crashing down when Axter’s husband, Ryan Bingham, filed for divorce on June 29, 2019. 

Anna Axster’s Children

Anna Axster is a mother of three. Her ex-husband, Bingham, announced at his performance in Austin, Texas, on March 5, 2015, that he and his wife were expecting their first child in the summer of 2015. In his song “Broken Heart Tattoo” from his album “Fear and Saturday Night,” the talented singer reflects on what to say to his unborn child.

Since the announcement in 2015, the now-divorced couple has welcomed two more children. However, aside from disclosing they have three children, they did not reveal details about them to the public.  

Did Anna Axster Remarry?

Since the former couple parted ways, the stunning mother of three has returned to her low-profile life away from the media. Anna Axster is not in any relationship, and it is unknown if she plans to remarry. 

However, Bingham debuted his new romance with “Yellowstone” actress Hassie Harrison with an Instagram photo. The picture depicts the two kissing against the backdrop of a wood fire.

Like actor Pablo Schreiber’s ex-wife, Jessica Monty, Anna Axster is focusing on her career, and she lives a comfortable life with her three kids.
