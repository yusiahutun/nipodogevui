Ayesha Curry shows off svelte figure in new Instagram snaps and prompts weight loss drug speculation

Ayesha Curry took to Instagram on Thursday to show off her trimmed-down figure in two stylish snaps.

The 33-year-old wife of NBA star Stephen Curry, 35 - who shared she lost 35lbs in January of this year - looked slimmer than ever in a stylish floor-length plunging black gown.

'Last week in Abu Dhabi with @forbes for the most amazing #forbes3050 International Women’s Day summit. Such an incredible event!' Ayesha captioned the stunning photos. 

The post quickly caused a stir among her 7.9M followers, who speculated whether she might have used the help of Hollywood's favorite new weight loss drug Ozempic to achieve the transformation. 

'Beautiful as always but looking too thin,' one concerned follower commented. While another one simply wrote, 'Ozempic queen.'

Ozempic and Wegovy have surged in popularity after becoming the first drugs proven to aid weight-loss.

The weight loss drugs — which are given as weekly injections for $1,000 a month — work by mimicking a hunger hormone in the body to trick people into thinking they are full.

Ayesha wore her hair braided and in an updo for the event. In one photo she was pictured from the front as she showed off the spectacular dress and her svelte frame.

Another shot saw her posing from the side with a hand on her hip. The beauty accessorized the look with a number of silver bangles on her wrists. 

More fans continued to express their amazement, with one writing, 'Wow, you lost a lot of weight.' 

Others appeared impressed by the change, writing, 'The weight loss though.'

The chef and cookbook author has been on a health journey for three years, and now that she has lost weight, she wants the definition for her body. 

'I've kind of been going through a health journey for the past three years now,' she told People.

'I've lost 35 lbs. over the past year, which I'm really excited about, but now I want that definition and I want to feel strong in my skin.'

The beauty also shared with People that she has to remind herself not to compare her fitness success to others, especially her NBA superstar husband, Stephen Curry.

The pair met when they attended the same church as teenagers. Their relationship only evolved into a romantic one once they reconnected years later, in 2008.

They got married in June 2011 and had their first child, Riley, 10, the following year. The couple then welcomed two more children, Ryan, seven, and Canon, four.

Ayesha admitted that she has learned not to be competitive with her health and fitness journey and remain happy with the progress she's already made, especially since she's had three children in the past 10 years.

She said: 'I'm married to a high-performance athlete who does it all. And I think I had to be honest with myself and realize that I can't do that and I don't have to do that to see the results that I want to see.

'I kind of had this inner competition that didn't exist. And so I was almost always defeated in whatever it was that I was doing because I couldn't do it to that level. I think I had to take a second, take a deep breath and just realize, do what you can, and that's enough.'
