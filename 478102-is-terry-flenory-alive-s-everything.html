Drug trafficking and money laundering

Southwest T and his brother Big Meech co-founded a drug trafficking and money laundering business known as the Black Mafia Family while they were in high school. The organisation was founded in 1985 in Detroit, Michigan, USA. The brothers were skilled enough to expand the illegal trade networks to other states in the United States.

Within a short time, the organisation had managed to employ over 500 employees whose work was to traffic thousands of kilograms of coc*aine in different states.

The significant states which benefited from the Black Mafia Family commodity included Kentucky, Georgia, Alabama, California, Michigan, Missouri, Florida, Louisiana, Mississippi, North Carolina, Ohio, and Tennessee.
