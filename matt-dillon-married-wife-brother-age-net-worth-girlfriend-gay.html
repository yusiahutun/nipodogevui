Matt Dillon Married, Wife, Brother, Age, Net Worth, Girlfriend, Gay

Matt Dillon is a very successful actor in many ways. His star shone in the 1980s, a period in which he quickly developed into a teenage heartbreaker who appeared in comedic and dramatic roles. When the new millennium arrived, it looked like Dillon had fallen off his horse, but like a superhero he returned to new glory in the mid-2000s, proving to movie lovers that he had never really lost his mojo. He had at least an Oscar nod and won a handful of other coveted awards.

Matt Dillon Bio (Age)

Matt Dillon was born Matthew Raymond Dillon on February 18, 1984, in New Rochelle, New York. He was born as the second of 6 children to Mary Ellen, a housewife, and Paul Dillon, a portrait painter who also worked for Union Camp as a sales manager. Dillon spent most of his childhood in Mamaroneck, New York, where he grew up as a Roman Catholic. His ethnic background is mainly Irish with traces of German and Scottish.

In the most unusual of fashions, Dillon was discovered by the cast leaders of the teen drama “Over the Edge” when he was playing hooky at Hommocks Middle School. He got a role in the film that became his debut. Dillon’s performance was acclaimed by the critics, and without further proof, Dillon decided to pursue an acting career.

As a somewhat stubborn teenager in IRL, Dillon drew on personal experiences and played tough characters, most notably in the adaptation of the novels H.E. Hinton – Tex (1982), Rumble Fish (1983), and The Outsiders (1983). In the 1990s he shone with roles in To Die For (1995) and There’s Something About Mary (1998).

Dillon added directing and screenwriting to his repertoire and released his film City of Ghosts (2002). His career reached a new level thanks to his role in Crash (2004), which earned him Oscar and Golden Globe nominations. The actor continues to make unsurpassed contributions to the entertainment industry.

Matt’s Brother

The other Dillon, Kevin Dillon, who is making waves in Hollywood, is Matt’s younger brother. Kevin, who was born on August 19, 1965, is only one year younger than Matt. Like his brother, Kevin began his Hollywood career as a teenager and has built an enviable resume.

He has appeared in several movies and television shows but is best known for his role as Johnny “Drama” Chase in the HBO comedy series Entourage, which earned him 3 Primetime Emmy nominations.

Kevin Dillon’s other notable works include roles in the British-American war movie Platoon (1986) and the role of John Densmore in the musical biography The Doors (1991). Some of his recent efforts include recurring roles in How to Be a Gentleman, TripTank, and Blue Bloods.

Kevin Dillon was married to model and actress Jane Stuart from 2006 to 2017. Their union produced one child, daughter Ava, who was born in Los Angeles in May 2006. Kevin has another child, a daughter named Amy from a previous relationship.

Matt Dillon Net Worth

With a resume long enough to rival many actors, Matt Dillon is very successful in his acting career. His net worth is estimated at $40 million as of 2024.
