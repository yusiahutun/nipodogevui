The 6 Best Plumping Lip Glosses of 2024, Tested By Us

Plump, pouty lips are highly sought after these days, and with such a saturated market of lip-plumping devices, glosses, and treatments available both at home and in a pro's office, it's hard to know what option is right for you. If you're looking for a less-invasive way to plump your lips, plumping balms and glosses are a great place to start. To make achieving the perfect pout easier, we set out to find the best lip plumpers. We researched the top plumpers on the market and enlisted the help of a handful of reviewers to test out the most popular options firsthand.

After thoroughly testing 27 lip plumping balms, glosses, and serums — taking into account comfort, plumping power, and hydration — we determined the top six lip plumpers. In the end, the Patrick Ta Major Volume Plumping Lip Gloss proved to be the key to a plump pucker and scored high in the aforementioned categories.

If you're looking for the best lip plumpers to pump you up, these are the six top performers, according to InStyle testers.

What to Keep in Mind

Ingredients

When shopping for a lip plumper, it's important to look at the ingredient label because the lips (and the area around them) are very delicate. "Seek out plumpers that utilize hyaluronic acid and collagen, as both ingredients help draw hydration to the top level of the skin to naturally plump and define the lips," says Brian Nourian, co-founder of Le Jolie Medi Spa in West Hollywood. He recommends avoiding harsh ingredients like peppermint oil, bee venom, and pepper extract because they boost lip shape by irritating the skin to increase blood flow to the surface. While it will give you results, it can harm your skin in the process.

Board-certified dermatologist Amelia K. Hausauer, M.D. agrees, stating that, "The products that cause vasodilation (the opening of blood vessels) from increased circulation can often cause irritation, redness, tingling or even an uncomfortable sensation."

Longevity

How long your newly plumped lips will last depends on the product. Some products boast 24 hour longwear, while others can resemble the effects of filler when applied consistently over time. When looking at lasting power, think about the occasion or outings you'll want to wear plumper to and consider your personal preference. Usually, the longer the effect will last, the better, so you don't need to worry about reapplying. A product with color may also need to be applied more often to maintain its vibrancy.

Your Questions, Answered

What are the benefits of a lip plumper?

If you're new to the world of lip plumpers and are considering giving one a try, know that there can be many benefits to applying a swipe or two everyday or every once in a while. "Lip plumpers can add an instant boost of fullness and shine, especially for special events as there is no downtime after using them and they offer instant results," Nourian says. "If you're considering getting lip fillers, lip plumpers are a nice, non-committal way to enhance your lip shape and see what your lips might look like."

Are lip plumpers safe?

Lip plumpers rely on humectants, like hyaluronic acid, and are safe to use everyday. Dr. Hausauer notes that these products are made even better when combined with emollients, like ceramides, and occlusives, like squalane and petroleum jelly. "This trifecta is the hallmark of great lip moisturizers and are safe to use on the gentle lip skin as often as necessary," she says. She warns, though, that lip plumpers that offer a swelling effect should be used in moderation.

How often should you apply lip plumpers?

The frequency at which you should apply lip plumpers will depend on the product and its formulation. "Products that rely on moisturizing ingredients are fine to use regularly, since they may improve the lip skin over time," Dr. Hausauer says. "Ones with harsher ingredients should be tested before wearing in order to eliminate irritation."
