Biography

Tim Kurkjian was born in Bethesda, MD on December 10, 1956. Baseball reporter best known for his decades of work as a Major League Baseball journalist and analyst with ESPN. He has authored several book on baseball, including Is This a Great Game, or What? (2007) and I’m Fascinated by Sacrifice Flies (2016). He has been a weekly contributor to The Dan Le Batard Show. He played baseball and basketball at Walter Johnson High School in Bethesda, Maryland. He later studied journalism at the University of Maryland.
