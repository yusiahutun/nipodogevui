Sandra Bullock Makes Rare Solo Appearance and Heads to the Hair Salon Have a Look!

Everyone needs a day for themselves, and that’s exactly what Sandra Bullock did recently when she was spotted heading to her hair appointment at the Nancy Braun salon in West Hollywood.

The 55-year-old was seen on Tuesday, September 10, wearing a gray maxi dress matched with the same-colored fuzzy slippers, as she made her way to the hair stylist. The actress also sported some sunglasses and tried to keep a low-profile — and that’s usually what the Oscar winner does while out and about. However, she has been seen with her loved ones a few times too.

The Bird Box star has been dating her boyfriend, Bryan Randall, since 2015. While the pair do not have any of their own children, Sandra does have two adopted kids — Louis, 9, and Laila, 7. And even though she may be a famous mom, her two little ones don’t see her any differently.

“They don’t really know that their mother is a huge movie star, they just know her as mommy,” an insider shared exclusively with Closer Weekly. “Louis and Laila are two of the most polite and well behaved children I know. There’s not an ounce of brattiness, what you might expect from Hollywood kids, and that’s because Sandra is nothing but a mom to them.” So great!
