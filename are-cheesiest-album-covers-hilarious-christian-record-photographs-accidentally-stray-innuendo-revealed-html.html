Hilarious Christian record photographs that often accidentally stray into innuendo

A collection of Christian album covers from decades gone by have surfaced...and they prove that time can be cruel.

The images, from albums released in the latter half of the 20th century, show family bands wearing matching attire, often looking like they've raided a local curtain shop. And the albums' titles reflect a far more innocent society five decades ago. 

While all-girl trio The Faith Tones might have thought they were cutting edge when they released their album 'Jesus Use Me' in 1964, their cheery album cover showing them with carefully coiffured hair and matching blue jackets is the epitomy of dated in 2016.
