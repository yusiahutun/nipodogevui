Top 10 Most Popular Jordans of All Time

(Source-Luxe-Digital)

Are you a sneaker lover? Do you love collecting branded shoes? One of the popular sneaker brands is Air Jordan which is popular not only among common people but also among players and athletes. Jordans are more than footwear. They are not only fashion statements but also symbols of style, status, and personal expression.

Here we are going to list down the top 10 most popular Jordans of all time.

Jordans was first released in 1985 by Michael Jordan, a famous basketball player. He had signed a five-year endorsement deal with Nike and released its first shoe, Air Jordan 1, which is still popular to this day. In 1986, the Air Jordan II was introduced to the world. But it wasn’t until 1988 that Air Jordans truly became iconic. A talented architect turned designer named Tinker Hatfield made a bold move. He decided to remove the Nike Swoosh from the shoes and replace it with a new logo called the ‘Jumpman’. 

This simple change transformed the Air Jordans from being just basketball shoes to becoming a must-have fashion statement for the streets. From that moment on, Air Jordans became more than just athletic footwear – they became a symbol of style and culture. Through this article, let’s explore the most popular Jordans of all time.

1. Jordan 1 ‘Chicago’

The Air Jordan 1 Retro Chicagois one of the most popular Jordans and was last released in 2015, and is a dream come true for collectors. However, they are not easy to find because they have become a must-have for street ballers and basketball fans. Since appearing on The Last Dance, a popular documentary series, the Chicago color scheme has become incredibly popular. But if you have around AUD 4000 to spare, you can still get your hands on a pair of these iconic shoes.

2. Jordan 3 ‘Black Cement’

In 2018, The famous Jordan 3 Retro OG was released, marking a new chapter for the brand. What made these sneakers stand out was the return of the original ‘Nike Air’ branding on the Black Cement colorway, something that hadn’t been seen since 2001. These shoes are known for their iconic elephant-print design, which adds a unique touch. They were released on the 55th birthday of Michael Jordan. So, every pair of these sneakers carries a little celebration with them. 

3. Jordan 5 ‘Fire Red’

You might be surprised to learn that the 5 Fire Red Retro OG is actually one of the newest additions to the Air Jordan collection. It was released in early 2020 to celebrate the silhouette’s 30th anniversary. This version of the shoe looks almost identical to the original pair from 1990. It has a sleek white leather upper and a bold black midsole with Fire Red accents in the shape of shark teeth. These features have made it one of the most beloved, sought-after, and one of the most popular Jordans of all time.

4. Jordan 4 ‘Bred’

Some things never go out of style, and the Air Jordan 4, one of the most popular Jordans of all time is a perfect example of that. The ‘Black Cement’ or ‘Bred 4’ is a classic design that has stood the test of time. These shoes have a red sole, a black upper with grey accents, and a white midsole with black, white, and grey details. While they may look a bit chunkier compared to newer designs, there’s no denying that these Jordans are some of the most stylish and attractive ones ever made.

5. Jordan 11 ‘Concord’

In 1995, Michael Jordan made a big impact in the world of sneakers with a pair of Air Jordan XIs. These shoes were special because they had a unique design that no one had seen before. They had a shiny patent leather upper and a see-through sole. Even after more than 30 years, the Concord XI is still considered one of the greatest Air Jordan shoes ever made. And because of the number 45 on the back of the shoe, it has become a highly sought-after item for collectors.

6. Jordan 6 ‘Black Infrared’

When it comes to Air Jordan sneakers, the INFRARED color is considered the best. The Jordan 6 INFRARED has a black upper, black midsole with red accents and a see-through sole. This shoe is not only extremely popular but also holds a special significance. It’s the same shoe that Michael Jordan wore when he won his first championship in 1991. 

7. Jordan 1 Retro High ‘Pine Green’

Adding to the “Black Toe” collection, these Pine Green Retro high-top sneakers are not only considered some of the greatest Air Jordan shoes ever made, but they are also a favorite among Celtics fans. The upper part of this Jordan 1 Retro High is made from white tumbled leather. The design is completed with a black leather Swoosh, a sail-colored midsole, and a pine green outsole.

8. Air Jordan 3 Retro ‘UNC’

The 2020 edition of these classic Air Jordans has a white tumbled leather upper with grey elephant print mudguards and valor blue accents. These sneakers were originally made in a special colorway for students at the University of North Carolina but have since been made available to the public. If you’re looking to buy a pair, expect to pay around AUD 600.

9. Jordan 11 ‘Bred’

The AJ11 Bred is a legendary pair of Air Jordans that were first worn in April 1996 during NBA games. They are known for being playoff-ready and are considered one of the most sought-after Jordans that money can buy. The version mentioned here is the 2019 edition, which was the first release of this style in seven years. These shoes hold a special place among Michael Jordan’s most coveted items and are widely regarded as one of the greatest Air Jordan shoes ever made.

10. Air Jordan 4 Retro ‘What The’

To celebrate Michael Jordan’s 30th anniversary, a special edition of the Jordan sneakers called the “What The” colorway was released. These sneakers are considered one of the most attractive Jordans ever made. The blue AJ4 design combines different colors like white, cement, military blue, fire red, and black. The heel tabs also have the ‘Nike Air’ branding, adding a stylish touch to the shoes.

These were the top 10 most popular Jordans of all time that you must add to your collection of shoes. With their elegant design and color, these sneakers aren’t just iconic but a must-have as well.
