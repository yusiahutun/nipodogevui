Dennis Ombachi's Wife Lovingly Teaches Daughter to Stand on Her Own: &quot;Good Job&quot;

  • Kenyan rugby sevens player Dennis Ombachi shared a lovely video as his wife Svetlana Polikarpova taught their daughter to stand
  • Sasha, their second-born child after Remy, followed her mother's instructions as they enjoyed quality family time
  • At one point, Remy caused tantrums as he wanted to be carried, but his mum was busy with her daughter

Parents are responsible for most of their children's milestones, as their parenting skills can determine how quickly they reach those milestones.

Ombachi records daughter standing

Self-taught chef Dennis Ombachi was delighted with his daughter as she learned to stand on her own.

Ombachi's longtime lover Svetlana Polikarpova took the centre stage to help their little bundle of joy with the skills.

"Sasha milestones and Remy's tantrums," read the caption on the Instagram story by the happy father of two.

Sasha bonds with mum over standing exercises

Sasha obediently followed her mother's instructions as they happily bonded in their beautiful home.

Polikarpova would occasionally squat like her daughter, then slowly rise to lift her hands up.

Beautiful Sasha, the second born of the family, would also do the same as she learns to stand with her two feet independently.

As they repeated the exercise, enjoying the mother-daughter time, Remy crawled to his jolly mother.

Remy causes tantrums behind mum

He then went behind her and put his arms around her neck, hoping his mum would carry him.

However, she was too preoccupied with her baby girl that she ignored her little boy who wanted to be lifted.

Remy decided to cause tantrums as he lay on the floor with his belly and started crying.

His father calmly told him his sister was learning to stand and his mother was helping, so he shouldn't cry.

Ombachi celebrates daughter Tasha for standing

The boy laid quietly on his belly as Ombachi's lover continued to spend quality time with Sasha.

Ombachi could be heard congratulating his daughter as he recorded the session on his phone.

In the end, Sasha stood for a couple of seconds with her little arms wide open as she stared at the mum.

She then made quick baby steps towards the loving mother who was waiting to embrace her while squatting.

Dennis Ombachi throws son in pool

In another instance, Ombachi stunned netizens and received mixed reactions after dipping his son in the swimming pool.

Most netizens expressed their dissatisfaction, noting the technique he employed could have hurt the child.

"Four weeks since we started water safety classes and my son graduated today, officially water safe! Last class, he gets to go in with 4 layers of clothing, including two diapers, more than doubling his weight in water. Proud father," he tweeted.
