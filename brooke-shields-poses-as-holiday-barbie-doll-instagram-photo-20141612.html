Brooke Shields Poses as Holiday Barbie Doll: Instagram Photo

Ready, set, smize! Brooke Shields stood inside a life-size Barbie box in an amazing new photo posted to her Instagram on Monday, Dec. 15, where she posed as… herself!

“I made this,” the supermodel, 49, proudly wrote. “Thanks to you #ContainerStore!!”

In the snap, Shields is literally all-dolled up in a bedazzled emerald top, a flouncy skirt, and elaborate pearl drop earrings. The Lipstick Jungle actress’ porcelain look is complete with faux eyelashes and festive red lipstick, while her brunette tresses are styled in perfect curls.

Most noteworthy of all is Shields’ ability to perfectly mimic Barbie’s frozen smile as the wrapped, red glittered box’s plastic sheen encases her from head to toe.

The creepy life-size recreation is a reminder to all that Shields once had her own Barbie doll after she skyrocketed to fame as a child and teen star in the ’80s. In 1982, the then 16-year-old star introduced an 11.5-inch doll, designed in her likeness, at an NYC toy fair.

The figurine resembled Shields with the same brunette tresses, blue eyes, natural dewy makeup, and ’80s style clothing including a bright pink and blue turtleneck sweater. It was touted as “The World’s Most Glamorous Teenager.”
