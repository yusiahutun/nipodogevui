Jessica Biels Feather Dress Was Pulled Straight from the Runway

Jessica Biel rarely strays from her minimalist, laid-back style, and so her latest maximalist red-carpet look came as a welcome surprise.

The actress and husband Justin Timberlake attended the Children's Hospital Los Angeles Gala this Saturday. The event took place at the Barker Hangar in Santa Monica, California.

For the glamorous night out, Biel wore a black gown pulled straight from Giambattista Valli's fall 2022 couture runway. The piece featured layers of feathers separated by metallic silver stripes and silky white bows—including one on the bust, above the plunging neckline. The look was put together by stylists Rob Zangardi and Mariel Haenn.

The Valentine's Day star accessorized only with diamond stud earrings and kept her makeup bronzy and natural. Her hair was styled in a loose low bun with wavy strands framing her face.

Next to her, the "Cry Me a River" singer looked dapper in a black suit with silky details by Brunello Cucinelli, a white dress shirt, and a black bow tie and shoes.

Momodu Mansaray//Getty Images

Biel and Timberlake are celebrating 10 years of marriage later this month, on October 19. The pair wed in 2012 in an intimate ceremony at the Borgo Egnazia resort in Puglia, Italy. Now, they share two sons: Silas, 7, and Phineas, 2.

A few weeks ago, the couple went on a romantic holiday to Porto Ercole, on the Argentario Coast of Tuscany. There, they were seen swimming in the ocean, as well as relaxing and exchanging kisses at the Hotel Il Pellicano, where they stayed.
