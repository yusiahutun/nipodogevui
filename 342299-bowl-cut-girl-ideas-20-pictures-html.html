What does bowl cut mean?

The style is popularly known as a mushroom cut, and it was prevalent in the '70s and '80s. However, it is making a comeback in today's fashion. A regular bowl cut is the same length around the head and looks like an upside-down bowl.

When was the bowl cut popular?

Interestingly, the bowl cut was a typical style among the male population in the '70s and '80s. You could get it from a regular barber. Since the beginning of the 21 century, the style has been ridiculed and converted into memes all over the internet.
