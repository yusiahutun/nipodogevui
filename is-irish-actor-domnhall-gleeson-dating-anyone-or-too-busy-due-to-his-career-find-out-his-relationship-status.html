Domhnall Gleeson: Quick Facts

 

  • Born on 12 May 1983, in Dublin, Ireland.
  • He is the son of Mary and actor Brendan Gleeson.
  • He shares three brothers, Fergus, Brian (also an actor), and Rúairí.
  • Attended Malahide Community School. He graduated from the Dublin Institute of Technology.
  • He graduated from Dublin Institute of Technology with a degree in Media Arts.
  • Upon graduation, the actor started directing and writing stage plays.
  • Made his acting debut in the 2001 mini-series, Rebel Heart, alongside James D'Arcy and Paloma Baeza.
  • He also starred in the Academy Award-winning movie, Six Shooters.
  • Nominated for the Tony Award for his performance in the show The Lieutenant of Inishmore.
  • Won the Berlin International Film Festival Award, Irish Film and Television Awards, and Hamptons International Film Festival.
  • In 2018, he performed in a live-action adaptation of Peter Rabbit.
  • He has an estimated net worth of about $7 million at present.
