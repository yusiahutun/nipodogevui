1. Matching sports jerseys, July 2023

This July, while Beyoncé was in New Jersey for a concert performance, her and a not-so-little-any more Blue Ivy wore matching bedazzled sports jerseys with thigh-high camo boots while backstage at the show.

Earlier in July, the dynamic duo looked great together in colourful outfits. 4. Evening wear, October 2022

According to Elle, Blue Ivy bid on a pair of US$80,000 Lorraine Schwartz diamond earrings at the Wearable Art Gala after being pictured in a stunning royal blue suit with her mum for the event back in October 2022. The dolled-up duo both wore gloves for extra impact, with Beyoncé, ever the goddess, shining in a black-and-white gown with a long train.
