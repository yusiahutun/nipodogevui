Movie legend is only just getting a star in Hollywood after 30 years - it's insane

Movie legend Macaulay Culkin will finally be getting a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, over three decades since the film that catapulted him to stardom was released.

Macaulay Culkin, who played Kevin McCallister in Christmas classic Home Alone and its sequel, Home Alone: Lost in New York, will be receiving a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame next month. However, it’s 33 years after the first movie opened in theaters.

Macaulay Culkin finally receives a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame

The official Hollywood Walk of Fame Instagram account posted the news. A black and white picture of the movie star was used to commemorate the tribute.

It wrote: “Actor Macaulay Culkin will be honored with the 2,765th star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame on Friday, December 1st, at 11:30 a.m. PT. Star will be located at 6353 Hollywood Blvd. Culkin will receive his star in the category of Motion Pictures.”

They also shared that the emcee for the occasion will be Steve Nissen. Speakers at the event will include celebrities Catherine O’Hara and Natasha Lyonne.

Fans say it’s “well deserved”

Fans reacted to the post with excitement for the 43-year-old. Many agree that it’s been too long, and Macaulay Culkin should already have a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.

“It’s about time!! Go Mac!!!!!!” said one person, adding raised hands and a heart emoji.

Another penned: “Thought he already had one to be honest!”

A third wrote: “As he should.”

Macaulay has a family with a former Disney star

One of the strangest and yet cutest celebrity couples to have arisen in showbiz has to be Macaulay and Disney Channel actress Brenda Song. Brenda played London Tipton in The Suite of Zack and Cody and its sequel series, Suite Life on Deck. The pair have been together for six years and have two children together, a son, born in 2021, and another born last year in December. They also became engaged early in 2022.

A source told People: “Macaulay and Brenda have loved being together as a family since welcoming Dakota. The engagement is the natural next step for them. They are excited for their future together.”

When posting about her beau for his birthday in 2020, Brenda, 35, said: “Happy 40th birthday to this magical being. I could sit here and write endlessly about how wonderful, kind, loving, genuine, loyal, honest, brilliant and hilarious you are, and how grateful I am that I get to share and do this life thing with you,” she wrote.

“But firstly, that would take me an eternity and secondly, you won’t even see this because you don’t ever use Instagram. Hahaha. My unicorn that I never thought could exist, I am the luckiest person in the world because I am loved by you.”
