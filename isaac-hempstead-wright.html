Isaac Hempstead Wright (born Isaac William Hempstead; April 9, 1999) is an English actor. He is best known for his role as Bran Stark in the HBO series Game of Thrones (2011–2014, 2016–2019), which earned him a Young Artist Award nomination as Best Young Supporting Actor in a TV Series. He also voiced Eggs in the 2014 animated film The Boxtrolls.
