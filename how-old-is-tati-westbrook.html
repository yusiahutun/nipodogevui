What happened to Tati Westbrook?

Nothing unfortunate happened to Tati Westbrook. But, she was dragged into various controversies and was facing lawsuits. So, what happened was Mrs. Westbrook and James Charles were having some internal conflict which later began to show on their social media handles, including their YouTube Channel.

Tati’s net worth is supposed to be about $12million, according to the U.S. Sun. Her primary source of income is acquired from her YouTube Channel named Tati. Overall, we much say that Tati has established herself as a makeup and beauty expert and has been living an opulent lifestyle.
