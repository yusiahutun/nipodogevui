Kate Middleton's Baby-Blue Dress Is Her Prettiest Summer Look Yet

Pastels are dominating the Princess of Wales’s casual summer wardrobe this season.

Earlier today, Princess Kate opted for the soft palette while attending the Out-Sourcing Inc. Royal Charity Polo Cup 2023 at Guards Polo Club, where she looked pretty in a baby-blue midi dress from Beulah London. The feminine piece featured a delicate white floral print, a high neckline, and billowing blouson sleeves. She paired the dress with gold accents: a blue shoulder bag with a gold chain strap, a gold beaded bracelet, and gold hoop earrings. She finished off the look with a pair of amber-tinted sunglasses.

Today’s outfit follows a pastel trend the royal seems to have set this summer.

For instance, earlier this week, Kate appeared at the Wimbledon Championships in a mint-green Balmain blazer with a plunging V-neckline, white lapels, and matching white buttons. She color-coordinated with a flowing pleated white skirt, pearl drop earrings, and her go-to Gianvito Rossi pumps in an ivory hue.

Last month, the princess also headed out for a public engagement in a monochromatic pastel outfit. For the opening of London’s Young V&A museum of childhood, Kate re-wore a blush Beulah London dress she had last worn to Wimbledon in 2021. The pretty pink piece featured a midi silhouette, puffy short sleeves, a built-in belt, a high collar, pleated details at the torso, and tiny buttons down the center of the bodice. She capped off the look with white pumps and drop earrings then too.
