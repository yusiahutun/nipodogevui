Acrobat Falls During Garden Bros Circus Show (Video)

TOLEDO, Ohio (CelebrityAccess) An acrobat who was part of the Garden Bros Circus performance plummeted to the ground during his act, bringing the performance to a sudden halt at the Huntington Center in Toledo, Ohio, May 13.

The acrobat, who survived, slipped while doing a front flip on top of a spinning wheel and came crashing to the ground, causing the audience to scream.

He fortunately fell from a point that was not the highest, although spotters could not react in time.

Paramedics responded quickly and the performer was released from a hospital with minor injuries, according to TMZ. He is expected to return for tonight’s show.
