Fargo Brewing Company Adds 400-Cap Live Venue, Announces Shows

FARGO, N.D. (CelebrityAccess) Fargo Brewing Company, along with Jade Presents and Jade Nielsen Properties, has announced the renovation and expansion of the North Dakota brewing company including the addition of The Hall, a 400-capacity live venue.

The announcement also includes the introduction of two shows – The Allman Betts Band on Aug. 11 and Reverend Horton Heat with Delta Bombers Aug. 12.

The expansions are expected to roll out through the summer.

“In addition to enjoying their favorite FBC beer, patrons will now be able to stop in for a haircut, tattoo, or artisan espresso drink all under the same roof,” the company said in the announcement. The additions include a new-and-improved taproom, outdoor patio space, and year-round food truck.

The 4,200-square-foot Hall at Fargo Brewing Company is also expected to be utilized for other events like zumba sessions, yoga, weddings and private parties with 40 events expected annually.
