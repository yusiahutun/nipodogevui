Starbucks Launches Franken Frappuccino

Just in time for Halloween, Starbucks has launched its most monstrous drink to date: The Franken Frappuccino.

A rep for Starbucks confirmed to PEOPLE that through October 31, customers can score the green-tea based drink, which is made with peppermint syrup, white chocolate sauce and java chips, and topped with whipped cream and mocha drizzle. According to Reddit user Callthewindreddit, “It tastes like a Frappucino version of mint chocolate ice cream — super yummy.”
