Bio of Dave “Heavy D” Sparks – Age, Family

Dave Sparks, also known as Heavy D, is 38 years old. The social media personality was born on January 6, 1985, and is of mixed race.

Despite having German ancestors, the man is an American citizen. Dave is very proud of his ancestry and has taken steps to help those who are suffering as a result of their mixed family background.

Going over his family history, Dave discovered that his father had undergone extensive brain surgery due to a brain tumor. He didn’t stand a chance of surviving the tumor after Dave was born.

Dave’s father, on the other hand, lived for another 21 years due to his high self-motivation. As a result, Dave, who witnessed his father perform mobile car installations and pizza deliveries during his recovery period, considers him to be one of his greatest inspirations.

Dave also refers to his mother as everyone’s best friend and the patron saint of women. Dave is the fourth child of his mother, who has always been the family’s rock. She is a source of strength not only for her children but also for her husband, who is battling cancer.

Following that, Dave attributes his success to his mother, who instilled in his persistence and confidence.
