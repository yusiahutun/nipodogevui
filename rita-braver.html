Rita Braver Biography, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Rita Braver is an American personality who is a television presenter, and news correspondent and is currently working for CBS news. She is famously known for being an investigative journalist of White House scandals such as the Iran-Contra affair.

Early Life

Rita Braver was born on 12th April 1948 and was raised in Silver Spring, Maryland. She lost her father at a very young age and was brought up mainly by her mother who raised her along with her two other siblings named Bettie Braver Sugar and Sharon Braver Cohen. She is a graduate student of the University of Wisconsin-Madison, from where she received her degree in Political Science.

Rita Braver Career

After completing her education, Braver spent a few years working for WWL-TV in New Orleans as a copy girl and after spending a few months, she moved to Washington, DC along with her husband and joined the network called CBS in 1972 at the position of Producer. For a brief period of 10 years (from 1983 to 1993), she served as the CBS News’ Chief law correspondent and worked on major stories like the John Walker spy ring, as well as that of another spy, Jonathan Pollard. While being with CBDS, she led to major coverage of the stories like the Iran-Contra affair. She has also worked as the Chief White House Correspondent for the CBS network during the administration of Bill Clinton and his first term.
