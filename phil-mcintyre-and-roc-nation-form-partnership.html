Phil McIntyre And Roc Nation Form Partnership

LOS ANGELES (VIP NEWS) — US based Roc Nation has partnered with Phil McIntyre`s management company Philymack.


Philymack`s roster includes artists such as Demi Lovato and Nick Jonas.


Philymack and Roc Nation will join forces across all areas of the music and entertainment business, although no more specific details about the partnership have been revealed yet.


"We sat down and dreamt about what the future could hold if we partnered," said Roc Nation founder Jay Z. "Our views aligned on nurturing, growth and allowing creatives to stay true to their voice. That dream we are living today and it`s been amazing for all parties. We look forward to what tomorrow brings."


Roc Nation CEO Jay Brown said: "We believe in Phil, his company and his vision. He is committed to and has a passion for developing talent. His character and business sense are reflective of the Roc Nation philosophy."
