Chloé Black Jamie Ankle Boots

Chloé’s Black Jamie Ankle Boots are now 70 percent off. While ankle boots have always been a consistent winter staple, I’m obsessed with how this style brings something new to the classic look. The Jamie bootie features a ribbed-knit ankle that gives the illusion of a chunky sock being folded over the top of the boot, while the small platform makes them a little more practical than a standard heeled style. Bonus: These are made with a calfskin exterior and leather lining. 

Jil Sander Black Open-Back Midi Dress

Jil Sander’s Black Open-Back Midi Dress is also 70 percent off during the biannual sale — and the piece might just be my top pick. The stretchy dress features a fitted top that extends into a flowing pleated skirt, as well as a scooped, open back that’s secured with a knotted detail at the shoulders. It’s the perfect balance of modest and sexy that makes it versatile enough to wear to work (blazer on) and date night (blazer off).

Marni Blue Mini Soft Trunk Bag

For a pop of color, I’m eyeing Marni’s 70-percent off crossbody bag in the most darling baby blue. The calfskin purse is designed with accordion paneling, giving you more space than meets the eye, plus hidden pockets and gold details that elevate the everyday style. 

Jacquemus Black Le Chouchou 'Le Pantalon Criollo' Trousers

These Jacquemus trousers are the definition of an elevated basic. The black slacks are high-waisted, wide-legged, and made of virgin wool. But what sets this style apart from a standard black pant — aside from the impeccable tailoring and luxe-feel — is the skinny belt that hits just below the hip. It’s a blink-and-you-miss-it detail but one that manages to make the wardrobe staple feel entirely fresh.

Isabel Marant Etoile Pink Drussell Sweater

This brushed mohair sweater from Isabel Marant Etoile is, IMO, another immediate buy. Red and pink have been neck-in-neck for 2024’s ‘It’ color title, and with this crew-neck knit, you get both. Plus, the mohair blend adds a unique, fuzz-like texture to the already eye-catching piece, making it far from a basic run-of-the-mill sweater.
