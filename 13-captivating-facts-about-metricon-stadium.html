A Modern Icon of Sports

Metricon Stadium, located on the Gold Coast in Queensland, Australia, stands as a modern icon in the world of sports and entertainment. With its state-of-the-art facilities and stunning architectural design, this stadium truly captivates visitors from all over the globe.

Home to the Gold Coast Suns

Metricon Stadium serves as the home ground for the Gold Coast Suns, an Australian rules football team competing in the Australian Football League (AFL). The stadium’s vibrant atmosphere and top-notch amenities create an unforgettable experience for both players and spectators alike.

Capacity and Size

As one of the largest stadiums in Australia, Metricon Stadium boasts an impressive capacity of approximately 25,000 seats. Its spacious grounds cover a total area of 223,567 square meters, providing ample space for various sports and entertainment events.

Multi-Purpose Venue

Metricon Stadium is not limited to hosting AFL matches only. It also serves as a versatile venue for a wide range of other events, including concerts, music festivals, rugby matches, and more. Its flexible design allows for seamless adaptation to different event requirements.

Green Initiatives

Metricon Stadium is committed to sustainability and environmental conservation. The stadium features innovative design elements such as solar panels, rainwater harvesting systems, and energy-efficient lighting, making it an eco-friendly landmark.

Major Sporting Events

Over the years, Metricon Stadium has played host to several major sporting events. It was a venue for the 2018 Commonwealth Games, showcasing world-class athletic competitions and creating lasting memories for athletes and spectators.

Concerts by Global Superstars

Besides sports events, Metricon Stadium has welcomed numerous international music artists and bands for unforgettable live performances. Renowned superstars like Ed Sheeran, Taylor Swift, and Coldplay have graced the stage, attracting thousands of fans.

Outstanding Facilities

Metricon Stadium offers exceptional facilities to enhance the spectator experience. These include premium seating areas, corporate suites, spacious concourses, food and beverage outlets, and modern restroom facilities, ensuring comfort and convenience for all visitors.

Picture-Perfect Location

Situated in the suburb of Carrara, Metricon Stadium enjoys a picturesque location surrounded by stunning landscapes and beautiful parks. It provides visitors with a tranquil escape from the bustling city, allowing them to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of the Gold Coast.
