Agent Christianne Weiss Signs With 33 &amp; West

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Independent talent agency 33 & West announced the addition of veteran music agent Christianne Weiss to the company.

As part of her multi-year agreement with 33 & West, Weiss’s longtime client, the Grammy-nominated band Starship, will join the company’s artist roster. Additional clients will be announced in the coming weeks.

“Christianne’s experience and talent as an agent fit right in with our vibrant office culture. She is a passionate agent with an outstanding reputation and truly cares for the artists she represents. She understands our agency’s big-picture vision and philosophies, and we are very excited to have her join our team,” said 33 & West Co-Founder Dan Rozenblum. “As an ever-evolving well-rounded agency, we are always looking to add talent to our company that propels us upward so that we can continually provide better services to our current and future clients,” added 33 & West Co-Founder Matt Pike.

“What 33 & West have successfully created in just a few years is so impressive,” says Weiss. “An independent agency operating at this high of a level, offering full services of music, touring, film, TV, comedy, and more, allows us to operate like a major and provide our clients with the ability to fulfill all their passions as an artist while maintaining the hands-on, white-glove agency-client relationship that the agency is built upon. I am thrilled to be part of the 33 & West team.”

The addition of Weiss to the team at 33 & West follows other recent additions at the company that include agent Daniel McCartney and tour marketing specialist Brandon Frankel.
