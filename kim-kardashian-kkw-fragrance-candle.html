Kim Kardashian is Launching a KKW Fragrance Candle

In her never-ending quest to make the world a better-smelling place, Kim Kardashian West just announced that she's launching her first candle under her KKW Fragrance brand. The Crystal Gardenia candle, launching September 27th, is inspired by her KKW perfume of the same name.

The candle will set you back $25 when it launches on September 27th on KKWFragrance.com. It will also be available in a bundle with the Crystal Gardenia perfume for $75, in case you need a refill.

The packaging for the candle is sleek and minimal. Housed in a clear glass jar, the cream-colored candle has the words KKW Fragrance written in white across the front. This is how the brand describes the candle on the site:

A voluptuous, lush and feminine fragrance. Crystal Gardenia offers a top note of water lily evoking a lush, wet petally effect to the opening. Timeless gardenia, tiara flower and velvet tuberose bring a trio of white florals to the heart offering a narcotic opulence and grand sillage. Warm woods and skin musk provide a contouring effect to the signature finish.

Kardashian West took to Instagram to announce the new product. "’m so excited to announce my new @kkwfragrance candle, inspired by one of my favorite scents Crystal Gardenia. I always have so many candles around my home and having a scent that illuminates the room makes me feel so good," she wrote. "You guys are going to love this candle!" Sure enough, fans are already spamming the comments letting her know how much they can't wait to get their hands on it—and requests for candles in her other scents too.
