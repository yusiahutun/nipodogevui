Cake in a Jar: 7 Deliciously Fun Recipes

Whether you’re throwing a birthday celebration, hosting a special gathering or just craving something sweet, cakes in a jar are the fun make-and-take desserts that couldn’t be easier to put together. Simply bake a batch of cupcakes, slice them in half horizontally, then layer in canning jars with frosting, fresh fruit, pie filling, sprinkles… the possibilities are endless! All those layers ensure you get some of the creamy frosting and fillings with every bite of cake. What could be better than that?

How to make the best-ever cake in a jar

One of the best things about cakes in a jar? You can make them as easy or as elaborate as you want — whether that’s using already-made cupcakes and frosting from the store, or making everything from scratch. First for Women test kitchen director Susan Chiusano likes to keep hers simple by starting with boxed cake mix, then pairing it with super-easy homemade buttercream frosting. To do, simply beat 1 lb. of butter (at room temp.) until fluffy, then beat in 4 cups of confectioners’ sugar until light and fluffy. It’s perfect as is, but you can also customize it by stirring in citrus zest, a little melted chocolate or your favorite extract—or add a drop of food coloring to really make it pop!
