A French actress and model, Eva Green manages to stay in the limelight of media because of her relationship. Eva mostly hooks up with actor and director and that invites her to become the headlines of tabloids. Let’s find out her past relation and current affair in our story.

Her Past Relationship With Marton:

If you are a Hollywood fan you may not have missed watching movie “kingdom of heaven”, which released in 2005.  The actors, Eva Green, and Marton Csokas, met on the set of the movie. Soon after, Eva and Marton started dating each other from 2005.

The couple stayed in a long distance relationship for three years after the completion of the movie. In 2008, Marton moved to London in order to stay with his darling, Eva. But sadly, the following year they broke the relation and got separated.

Who is she Currently Dating? Boyfriend?

After the separation with Marton, there was a long silence in her love life.  In 2015, she resumed romance in her life dating her director for “Miss Peregrine”, Tim Burton. Tim Burton was previously married to Harry Potter actor, Helena Bonham Carter but they separated in late 2014.  Tim and his ex-wife stayed in relation for 13 and had two children.

Although Eva and Tim have an age gap of about 22 years, they are going smooth in the relation. Tim shared the impression he created about Eva after their first meeting in an interview with esquire.com.  Tim believes that Eva is far beautiful inwardly than her outer beauty. He also confessed about their typical introvert nature. He said,

“Eva was not instantly knowable. There was something private and mysterious about her, and that’s not common in this day and age. People think she’s ‘dark,’ but she’s more interesting than that. People assume I’m dark too, but I’m really not. Is it just because I wear dark clothing? Do people want me to be in a white suit, like Mr. Roarke from Fantasy Island?! Eva faces the same dilemma. We share a complicated way of looking at things. We both have an interest in the unusual.”

Eva Green's Short Bio:

Born on 5 July 1980, Eva Green started her career as a theater artist before entering in movies. She made her debut with the controversial Bernardo Bertolucci’s film “The Dreamers”.  She has acted in a various film including “Casino Royal”, “kingdom of Heaven”, “Dark Shadows “and “cracks”.

Similarly, she has worked on television series “Chamelot” and “Penny Dreadful”. An outstanding actress, Eva has received many awards including “Saturn awards”, “Empire awards”, “BAFTA Awards” and “Fangoria Chainsaw Awards”. She is currently working on her movie Europhic, releasing in 2017.
