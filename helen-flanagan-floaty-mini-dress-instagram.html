Helen Flanagan's floaty mini dress has fans saying the same thing

The former Coronation Street star looked incredible

    Former actress Helen Flanagan, 31, has delighted her one million Instagram fans by sharing her new summer look – the prettiest mini dress.

The light pink floral design of the dress complemented Helen's beauty look – which she kept natural by sticking to similar pink and nude hues. Fans were in awe and gushed over the summery look.
