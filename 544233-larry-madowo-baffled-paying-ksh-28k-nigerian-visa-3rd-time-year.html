Larry Madowo Baffled after Paying KSh 28k for Nigerian Visa: &quot;3rd Time This Year&quot;

  • CNN reporter and anchor Larry Madowo has revealed the amount of money he pays for a visa each time he visits Nigeria
  • The journalist said he visited Nigeria thrice in 2024, and each time, he paid $215 (KSh 28k) for a single entry visa
  • He also said each time he visits, the authorities would take his fingerprints even though his fingerprints remain the same

Celebrated journalist Larry Madowo shared his experience after he visited Nigeria three times in 2024.

Madowo, who works with CNN, said that each time he takes a trip to Nigeria, he is charged $215 (about KSh 28k) for a single-entry visa.

He said that, for the three times he visited Nigeria in 2024, he had paid $645 (KSh 83k).

Madowo also said that the authorities took his fingerprints each time he applied for the visa, even though they remained the same.
