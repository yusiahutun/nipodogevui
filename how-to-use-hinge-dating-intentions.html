How to Use Hinge's Dating Intentions to Narrow Your Matches

Hinge is known for being an honest and upfront dating app, with its mantra being “The dating app designed to be deleted”. And unlike other apps of this nature, it claims longer-lasting results for its users trying to find that special someone.

No matter what your intention is on the dating app, Hinge boasts a higher success rate than other popular dating apps like Tinder and Bumble. And features such as Dating Intentions, which lets you indicate what sort of relationship you're looking for, help with that.

What Is Hinge’s Dating Intentions Feature?

Hinge’s Dating Intentions feature is a way for users to find people looking for the same sort of relationship. And by using Dating Intentions, you can narrow down the number of potential dates the algorithm picks out for you. This should equate to a more successful outcome for Hinge users.

How to Use Hinge’s Dating Intentions Feature

In order to set up or edit your Dating Intentions on Hinge, you need to:

  • Click onto the profile icon at the bottom-right of the panel on the screen when you open Hinge.
  • Then, click on the pencil icon below your profile picture. This will allow you to edit your profile.
  • Scroll down to the My Virtues section, where you will find Dating Intentions.
  • When you get onto the Dating Intentions feature, you can pick out of the following options as to what exactly you’re looking for:
    • Close
    • Life Partner
    • Long-term relationship
    • Long-term relationship, open to short
    • Short-term relationship, open to long
    • Short-term relationship
    • Figuring out my dating goals
    • Prefer not to say

    Once you have chosen the one best suited to you, you have the option to keep your intentions hidden on your profile. This is available at the bottom of the page of Dating Intentions.

    Whether you choose to have your intentions visible on your profile or not, setting them will increase your chances of finding someone on the same wavelength as you. It's a way to be even more upfront on Hinge and make the online dating process a little easier.

    How Hinge's Dating Intentions Could Make Dating Easier

    As previously mentioned, using the Dating Intentions feature increases the chances of finding compatible matches.

    Since its initial release in 2012, Hinge ran beta trials which resulted in “90% of members [who] said their first dates were great, and 72% said they wanted to go on a second,” according to TechCrunch.

    So far, there have been mostly positive reviews of the Dating Intentions feature, with the occasional user pointing out that Hinge was made for those looking for something of a longer-term relationship.

    If Hinge continues at the rate it's going, this feature could boost the app's popularity, as it's currently in third place behind Bumble and Tinder. Hinge is an app where you can make your profile stand out as much as possible, especially by adjusting your preferences and perhaps taking the perfect online dating profile picture.

    For the younger demographic that it’s aimed at, Hinge is a good way of meeting people. And since the Dating Intentions feature allows you to skip the awkward conversation of realizing that each person has different intentions, this could end up revolutionizing dating apps in the future. Also, Hinge verifies users through phone numbers, so it is unlikely you'll match with a fake dating profile.

    You Can Now Be Upfront With Your Dating Intentions on Hinge

    In order to boost your Hinge profile and find better suited matches, setting up your Dating Intentions would be a smart move.

    One of the benefits of online dating is that you can compare important traits with a potential match immediately. So the more info you know about someone, the more likely you are to enjoy a good first date. And more besides, if that's what you're looking for.
