Halle Bailey stays warm in a puffer coat while greeting fans outside Jimmy Kimmel Live... after clap

After thanking fans for their support while also warning haters not to comment about her 'pregnancy nose,' Halle Bailey was spotted at Jimmy Kimmel Live.
