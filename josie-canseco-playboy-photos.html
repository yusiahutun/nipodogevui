Jose Canseco's 19-Year-Old Daughter, Josie, Goes Bottomless for Playboy

Josie Canseco is following in her mom's half-naked footsteps.

Jessica Canseco, ex-wife of baseball superstar Jose Canseco, famously posed for Playboy magazine back in 2005 -- and now, the couple's 19-year-old daughter is stepping up to the plate.

The teen smolders as the mag's Miss June 2016, appearing in various states of undress for a 14-page pictorial and Centerfold inside the new issue.

"I'm so over people asking, 'Are you related to the baseball player?'" she tells the mag. "My family name has a lot of talk behind it. I believe one of my biggest achievements to date is maintaining my career and image through all the chaos."

That career includes modeling gigs, an appearance in a Mike Stud music video and co-starring with her mother on the reality show, "The Mother-Daughter Experiment: Celebrity Edition."

Courtesy Playboy/Henrik Purienne

"I didn't learn anything surprising about myself, because I don't want to be a reality star," she says of her time on the Lifetime series. "I'm actually a weird, goofy and friendly person. Giving in to drama simply isn't me."

So ... what does she enjoy doing?

"After a week of going to castings, running errands and doing hour-long sessions at the gym, I like to come home, throw on my sweats and turn on 'Friends' or 'Family Guy,'" she admits. "In fact, if I had to pick one nonessential item to have in my earthquake emergency kit, a pair of my favorite sweatpants would definitely be it."

She's definitely not wearing sweats in this shoot though!

"I would love to own a dance studio for all ages where I can teach kids," Canseco adds of her future plans. "After all, seeing my own family happy and healthy is one thing that will always make me happy in return."

See more ... a lot more ... of Josie in the June issue of Playboy, on newsstands now!
