Michelle Obama's Fashion Evolution in Over 100 Looks

In case you've missed it, Michelle Obama is not playing any games when it comes to her style. Not only has she been an icon since she stepped into the White House, but the former First Lady was also serving up jaw-dropping looks back-to-back while promoting her book Becoming several years later in 2019. We're still trying to catch our breath after seeing her hit the stage with Sarah Jessica wearing those thigh-high Balenciaga boots. And who could forget how classy she looked in that white suit with those adorable Jimmy Choo cage booties?

But Michelle's style hasn't always leaned toward the trendy side. Remember when Barack was in office? While in the White House, Michelle stuck to timeless closet staples, but now we're getting a chance to see her experiment more, showing off her personality through her outfit choices — and we're here for it.

See exactly how Michelle Obama's style has evolved by taking a look back at her best fashion moments over the years.

2022 - Teal Suit

Michelle Obama opted for a teal suit with a jumbo flower boutonniere, completing her look with a pair of metallic pointed-toe sling-backs on Jimmy Kimmel Live!

2022 - Leather Suit

Dressed in head-to-toe leather, Obama looks more like a street-style star than a former first lady. We are also loving the details of this look, from her studded boots to her face-framing tendrils.

2022 - Head-To-Toe Denim

As part of her The Light We Carry tour, Obama made a stop in D.C. wearing this denim-on-denim look. It's fashion-forward without trying too hard.

2022 - Three-Piece Suit

The former first lady gives the three-piece suit a modern-day makeover with bright, bold color. If menswear ever met Barbiecore we're pretty sure it would look something like this.
