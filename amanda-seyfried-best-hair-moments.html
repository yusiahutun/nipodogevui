Top 10 times Amanda Seyfried gave us major hair goals

    From edgy plaited hairstyles to boho chic curls, Amanda Seyfried always turns heads on the red carpet with her effortlessly glamorous hairstyles. We take a look at the top ten times the star gave us serious hair envy…

    Amanda proved a ponytail can be taken from day to night when she wore her locks swept back with the roots smoothed down and the ponytail left wavy for a red carpet-ready look.

    A chignon makes for instant glamour on any occasion, and the actress turned heads with her smooth ballerina bun as she arrived for the CFDA awards.

    The star mixed French plaits and braids for an effortlessly cool twist on a classic low bun.

    Amanda showed off her edgy side with this cool braided style – to get the look, section your hair and just braid the top sections, keeping the rest of your locks poker straight.

    The actress could have just stepped out of old Hollywood as she headed to New York Fashion Week with her tresses styled into retro waves with a dramatic side parting.

    Looking as elegant as ever, Amanda left a few hair strands loose as she sported a low chignon for a laidback chic finish.

    The blonde beauty topped beauty lists with the chic braided updo with heaps of volume which she sported for the Oscars.

    The young star was the epitome of boho chic with her long tresses worn down in voluminous curls, teamed with a small plait to complement the side parting.

    Amanda was an ethereal beauty for the Golden Globes with her long locks worn down in tumbling waves, parted in the middle for a classic red carpet look.

    The Hollywood starlet did effortless glamour for the K.I.D.S/Fashion Delivers Annual Gala with a sleek blow dry, the ends left in loose curls to show off her locks' layers.
